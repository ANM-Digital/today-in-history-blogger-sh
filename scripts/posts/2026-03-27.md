# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 1309 – Pope Clement V imposes excommunication and interdiction on Venice, and a general prohibition of all commercial intercourse with Venice, which had seized Ferrara, a papal fiefdom.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Clement_V)

🏰 1513 – Spanish explorer Juan Ponce de León reaches the northern end of The Bahamas on his first voyage to Florida.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juan_Ponce_de_Le%C3%B3n)

📜 1625 – Charles I becomes King of England, Scotland and Ireland as well as claiming the title King of France.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_I_of_England)

🛡️ 1782 – The Second Rockingham ministry assumes office in Great Britain and begins negotiations to end the American War of Independence.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Rockingham_ministry)

⚓ 1866 – President of the United States of America Andrew Johnson vetoes the Civil Rights Act of 1866. His veto is overridden by Congress and the bill passes into law on April 9.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States)

🚀 1901 – Philippine–American War: Emilio Aguinaldo, leader of the First Philippine Republic, is captured by the Americans.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine%E2%80%93American_War)

💼 1958 – Nikita Khrushchev becomes Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikita_Khrushchev)

🎖️ 1976 – The first section of the Washington Metro opens to the public.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_Metro)

📡 1975 – Construction of the Trans-Alaska Pipeline System begins.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trans-Alaska_Pipeline_System)

🏙️ 1993 – Jiang Zemin is appointed President of the People's Republic of China.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jiang_Zemin)

⚙️ 1998 – The Food and Drug Administration approves Viagra for use as a treatment for erectile dysfunction, the first pill to be approved for this condition in the United States.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Food_and_Drug_Administration)

💥 1980 – The Norwegian oil platform Alexander L. Kielland collapses in the North Sea, killing 123 of its crew of 212.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_L._Kielland_(platform))

🕊️ 1814 – War of 1812: In central Alabama, U.S. forces under General Andrew Jackson defeat the Creek at the Battle of Horseshoe Bend.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_1812)

⚔️ 2016 – A suicide blast in Gulshan-e-Iqbal Park, Lahore claims over 70 lives and leaves almost 300 others injured. The target of the bombing are Christians celebrating Easter.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2016_Lahore_suicide_bombing)

🏰 2000 – A Phillips Petroleum plant explosion in Pasadena, Texas kills one person and injures 71 others.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2000_Phillips_explosion)
