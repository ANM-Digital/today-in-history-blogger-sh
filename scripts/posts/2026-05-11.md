# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 330 – Constantine the Great dedicates the much-expanded and rebuilt city of Byzantium, changing its name to New Rome and declaring it the new capital of the Eastern Roman Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantine_the_Great)

🏰 868 – A copy of the Diamond Sūtra is published, the earliest dated and printed book known.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diamond_Sutra)

📜 973 – In the first coronation ceremony ever held for an English monarch, Edgar the Peaceful is crowned King of England, having ruled since 959 AD. His wife, Ælfthryth, is crowned queen, the first recorded coronation for a Queen of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edgar%2C_King_of_England)

🛡️ 1068 – Matilda of Flanders, wife of William the Conqueror, is crowned Queen of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matilda_of_Flanders)

⚓ 1258 – Louis IX of France and James I of Aragon sign the Treaty of Corbeil, renouncing claims of feudal overlordship in one another's territories and separating the House of Barcelona from the politics of France.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_IX_of_France)

🚀 1560 – The Ottoman naval forces inflict a defeat on a Spanish fleet in the battle of Djerba. This marks the highpoint of Ottoman naval dominance in the Mediterranean, which starts declining five years later after the failed siege of Malta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Navy)

💼 1713 – Great Northern War: After losing the Battle of Helsinki to the Russians, the Swedish and Finnish troops burn the entire city, so that it would not remain intact in the hands of the Russians.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Northern_War)

🎖️ 1919 – Uruguay becomes a signatory to the Buenos Aires copyright treaty.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uruguay)

📡 1813 – William Lawson, Gregory Blaxland and William Wentworth discover a route across the Blue Mountains, opening up inland Australia to settlement.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Lawson_(explorer))

🏙️ 1812 – Prime Minister Spencer Perceval is assassinated by John Bellingham in the lobby of the British House of Commons.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spencer_Perceval)

⚙️ 1996 – After the aircraft's departure from Miami, a fire started by improperly handled chemical oxygen generators in the cargo hold of Atlanta-bound ValuJet Airlines Flight 592 causes the Douglas DC-9 to crash in the Florida Everglades, killing all 110 on board.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ValuJet_Flight_592)

💥 1889 – An attack upon a U.S. Army paymaster and escort results in the theft of over $28,000 and the award of two Medals of Honor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wham_Paymaster_robbery)

🕊️ 1987 – Klaus Barbie goes on trial in Lyon for war crimes committed during World War II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Klaus_Barbie)

⚔️ 2011 – The Istanbul Convention is signed in Istanbul, Turkey.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Istanbul_Convention)

🏰 2009 – Space Shuttle Atlantis is launched on the final mission to service the Hubble Space Telescope.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Atlantis)
