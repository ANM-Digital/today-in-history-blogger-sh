# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -241 – First Punic War: Battle of the Aegates: The Romans sink the Carthaginian fleet bringing the First Punic War to an end.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Punic_War)

🏰 298 – Roman Emperor Maximian concludes his campaign in North Africa and makes a triumphal entry into Carthage.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maximian)

📜 947 – The Later Han is founded by Liu Zhiyuan. He declares himself emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Later_Han_(Five_Dynasties))

🛡️ 1496 – After establishing the city of Santo Domingo, Christopher Columbus departs for Spain, leaving his brother in command.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Santo_Domingo)

⚓ 1535 – Spaniard Fray Tomás de Berlanga, the fourth Bishop of Panama, discovers the Galápagos Islands by chance on his way to Peru.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom%C3%A1s_de_Berlanga)

🚀 1607 – Susenyos I defeats the combined armies of Yaqob and Abuna Petros II at the Battle of Gol in Gojjam, making him Emperor of Ethiopia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Susenyos_I)

💼 1735 – An agreement between Nader Shah and Russia is signed near Ganja, Azerbaijan and Russian troops are withdrawn from occupied territories.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nader_Shah)

🎖️ 1949 – Mildred Gillars ("Axis Sally") is convicted of treason.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mildred_Gillars)

📡 2006 – The Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter arrives at Mars.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mars_Reconnaissance_Orbiter)

🏙️ 1975 – Vietnam War: Ho Chi Minh Campaign: North Vietnamese troops attack Ban Mê Thuột in the South on their way to capturing Saigon in the final push for victory over South Vietnam.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1975_spring_offensive)

⚙️ 1922 – Mahatma Gandhi is arrested in India, tried for sedition, and sentenced to six years in prison, only to be released after nearly two years for an appendicitis operation.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahatma_Gandhi)

💥 1891 – Almon Strowger patents the Strowger switch, a device which led to the automation of telephone circuit switching.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Almon_Brown_Strowger)

🕊️ 1974 – 1974 Belgian general election: Elections are held in Belgium for all 212 seats in the Chamber of Representatives, the Belgian Socialist Party taking the majority with 59.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1974_Belgian_general_election)

⚔️ 2019 – Ethiopian Airlines Flight 302, a Boeing 737 MAX, crashes shortly after take off, killing all 157 passengers and crew. This and the prior Lion Air Flight 610 led to all 387 Boeing 737 MAX aircraft being grounded worldwide.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethiopian_Airlines_Flight_302)

🏰 1848 – The Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo is ratified by the United States Senate, ending the Mexican–American War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Guadalupe_Hidalgo)
