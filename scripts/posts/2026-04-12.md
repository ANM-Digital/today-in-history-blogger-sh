# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 240 – Shapur I becomes co-emperor of the Sasanian Empire with his father Ardashir I.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shapur_I)

🏰 467 – Anthemius is elevated to Emperor of the Western Roman Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anthemius)

📜 627 – King Edwin of Northumbria is converted to Christianity by Paulinus, Bishop of York.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edwin_of_Northumbria)

🛡️ 806 – Nikephoros I of Constantinople is consecrated as patriarch of Constantinople..

⚓ 1012 – Duke Oldřich of Bohemia deposes and blinds his brother Jaromír, who flees to Poland.[citation needed]. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old%C5%99ich%2C_Duke_of_Bohemia)

🚀 1159 – Having received the submission of the prince of Antioch, Raynald of Châtillon, Byzantine Emperor Manuel I Komnenos enters triumphantly the city of Antioch.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_of_Antioch)

💼 1204 – The Crusaders of the Fourth Crusade breach the walls of Constantinople and enter the city, which they completely occupy the following day.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crusades)

🎖️ 1606 – The Union Flag is adopted as the flag of English and Scottish ships.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Union_Jack)

📡 1776 – American Revolution: With the Halifax Resolves, the North Carolina Provincial Congress authorizes its Congressional delegation to vote for independence from Britain.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolution)

🏙️ 1985 – Space Shuttle Discovery launches on STS-51D to deploy two communications satellites.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Discovery)

⚙️ 1934 – The U.S. Auto-Lite strike begins, culminating in a five-day melee between Ohio National Guard troops and 6,000 strikers and picketers.[citation needed]. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Auto-Lite_strike_(1934))

💥 1862 – American Civil War: The Andrews Raid (the Great Locomotive Chase) occurs, starting from Big Shanty, Georgia (now Kennesaw).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Locomotive_Chase)

🕊️ 1807 – The Froberg mutiny on Malta ends when the remaining mutineers blow up the magazine of Fort Ricasoli.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Froberg_mutiny)

⚔️ 1865 – American Civil War: Mobile, Alabama, falls to the Union Army.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile%2C_Alabama)

🏰 1980 – Canadian runner and athlete, Terry Fox begins his Marathon of Hope Run in St. John's, NF. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terry_Fox)
