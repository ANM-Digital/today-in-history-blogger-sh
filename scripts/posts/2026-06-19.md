# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 325 – The original Nicene Creed is adopted at the First Council of Nicaea.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicene_Creed)

🏰 978 – Byzantine forces loyal to emperor Basil II under Bardas Phokas the Younger defeat rebel forces under Bardas Skleros in the battle of Pankaleia (approximate date).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Basil_II)

📜 1179 – The Battle of Kalvskinnet takes place outside Nidaros (now Trondheim), Norway. Earl Erling Skakke is killed, and the battle changes the tide of the civil wars.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trondheim)

🛡️ 1306 – The Earl of Pembroke's army defeats Bruce's Scottish army at the Battle of Methven.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aymer_de_Valence%2C_2nd_Earl_of_Pembroke)

⚓ 1586 – English colonists leave Roanoke Island, after failing to establish England's first permanent settlement in North America.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roanoke_Island)

🚀 1718 – At least 73,000 people die in the 1718 Tongwei–Gansu earthquake due to landslides in the Qing dynasty.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1718_Tongwei%E2%80%93Gansu_earthquake)

💼 1990 – The Communist Party of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic is founded in Moscow.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Communist_Party_of_the_Russian_Soviet_Federative_Socialist_Republic)

🎖️ 1991 – The last Soviet army units in Hungary are withdrawn.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Group_of_Forces)

📡 2007 – The al-Khilani Mosque bombing in Baghdad leaves 78 people dead and another 218 injured.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2007_al-Khilani_mosque_bombing)

🏙️ 1811 – The Carlton House Fête is held in London to celebrate the establishment of the Regency era.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carlton_House_F%C3%AAte)

⚙️ 1926 – King Roger, an opera about Roger II of Sicily by Karol Szymanowski, is premiered at the Grand Theatre in Warsaw.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_Roger_(opera))

💥 1947 – Pan Am Flight 121 crashes in the Syrian Desert near Mayadin, Syria, killing 15 and injuring 21.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pan_Am_Flight_121)

🕊️ 2009 – Mass riots involving over 10,000 people and 10,000 police officers break out in Shishou, China, over the dubious circumstances surrounding the death of a local chef.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shishou_incident)

⚔️ 1982 – The People's Armed Police is de facto founded; it is officially established 10 months later on April 5, 1983.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/People's_Armed_Police)

🏰 1960 – Charlotte Motor Speedway holds its first NASCAR race, the inaugural World 600.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charlotte_Motor_Speedway)
