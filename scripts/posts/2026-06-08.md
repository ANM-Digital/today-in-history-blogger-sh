# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 218 – Battle of Antioch: With the support of the Syrian legions, Elagabalus defeats the forces of emperor Macrinus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Antioch_(218))

🏰 452 – Attila leads a Hun army in the invasion of Italy, devastating the northern provinces as he heads for Rome.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attila)

📜 536 – Election of pope Silverius following the death of pope Agapetus I earlier that year.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Silverius)

🛡️ 793 – Vikings raid the abbey at Lindisfarne in Northumbria, commonly accepted as the beginning of Norse activity in the British Isles.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lindisfarne)

⚓ 1042 – Edward the Confessor becomes King of England – the country's penultimate Anglo-Saxon king.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_the_Confessor)

🚀 1191 – English forces under King Richard I arrive in Acre, joining the already arrived French and German forces of the Third Crusade.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_I_of_England)

💼 1663 – Portuguese Restoration War: Portuguese victory at the Battle of Ameixial ensures Portugal's independence from Spain.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portuguese_Restoration_War)

🎖️ 1772 – Alexander Fordyce flees to France to avoid debt repayment, triggering the credit crisis of 1772 in the British Empire and the Dutch Republic.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_Fordyce)

📡 1982 – VASP Flight 168 crashes in Pacatuba, Ceará, Brazil, killing 128 people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VASP_Flight_168)

🏙️ 1906 – Theodore Roosevelt signs the Antiquities Act into law, authorizing the President to restrict the use of certain parcels of public land with historical or conservation value.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodore_Roosevelt)

⚙️ 1867 – Coronation of Franz Joseph as King of Hungary following the Austro-Hungarian compromise (Ausgleich).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Franz_Joseph_I)

💥 1966 – An F-104 Starfighter collides with XB-70 Valkyrie prototype no. 2, destroying both aircraft during a photo shoot near Edwards Air Force Base. Joseph A. Walker, a NASA test pilot, and Carl Cross, a United States Air Force test pilot, are both killed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lockheed_F-104_Starfighter)

🕊️ 1959 – USS Barbero and the United States Postal Service attempt the delivery of mail via Missile Mail.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Barbero)

⚔️ 1992 – GP Express Airlines Flight 861 crashes on approach to Anniston Regional Airport in Anniston, Alabama, killing three.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GP_Express_Airlines_Flight_861)

🏰 1942 – World War II: The Imperial Japanese Navy submarines I-21 and I-24 shell the Australian cities of Sydney and Newcastle.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_Japanese_Navy)
