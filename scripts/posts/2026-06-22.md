# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -217 – Battle of Raphia: Ptolemy IV Philopator of Egypt defeats Antiochus III the Great of the Seleucid kingdom.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Raphia)

🏰 -168 – Battle of Pydna: Romans under Lucius Aemilius Paullus defeat Macedonian King Perseus who surrenders after the battle, ending the Third Macedonian War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Pydna)

📜 431 – The Council of Ephesus, the third ecumenical council, begins, dealing with Nestorianism.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Ephesus)

🛡️ 813 – Battle of Versinikia: The Bulgars led by Krum defeat the Byzantine army near Edirne. Emperor Michael I is forced to abdicate in favor of Leo V the Armenian.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Versinikia)

⚓ 910 – The Hungarians defeat the East Frankish army near the Rednitz River, killing its leader Gebhard, Duke of Lotharingia (Lorraine).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarians)

🚀 1527 – Fatahillah expels Portuguese forces from Sunda Kelapa, now regarded as the foundation of Jakarta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fatahillah)

💼 1633 – The Holy Office in Rome forces Galileo Galilei to recant his view that the Sun, not the Earth, is the center of the Universe in the form he presented it in, after heated controversy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dicastery_for_the_Doctrine_of_the_Faith)

🎖️ 1774 – The British pass the Quebec Act, setting out rules of governance for the colony of Quebec in British North America.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quebec_Act)

📡 1944 – U.S. President Franklin D. Roosevelt signs into law the Servicemen's Readjustment Act of 1944, commonly known as the G.I. Bill.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Franklin_D._Roosevelt)

🏙️ 2007 – The small town of Elie, Manitoba is hit by Canada's most intense tornado on record.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elie%2C_Manitoba)

⚙️ 1907 – The London Underground's Charing Cross, Euston and Hampstead Railway opens.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London_Underground)

💥 1978 – Charon, the first of Pluto's satellites to be discovered, was first seen at the United States Naval Observatory by James W. Christy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charon_(moon))

🕊️ 1922 – British Army Field Marshal Sir Henry Wilson is killed by the Irish Republican Army helping to spark the Irish Civil War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Field_marshal_(United_Kingdom))

⚔️ 2002 – An earthquake measuring 6.5 Mw strikes a region of northwestern Iran killing at least 261 people and injuring 1,300 others and eventually causing widespread public anger due to the slow official response.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moment_magnitude_scale)

🏰 1911 – Mexican Revolution: Government forces bring an end to the Magonista rebellion of 1911 in the Second Battle of Tijuana.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexican_Revolution)
