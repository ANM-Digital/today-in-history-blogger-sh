# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 421 – Emperor Theodosius II marries Aelia Eudocia at Constantinople (Byzantine Empire).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodosius_II)

🏰 879 – Pope John VIII recognises the Duchy of Croatia under Duke Branimir as an independent state.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_John_VIII)

📜 1002 – Henry II, a cousin of Emperor Otto III, is elected and crowned King of Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_II%2C_Holy_Roman_Emperor)

🛡️ 1420 – Troops of the Republic of Venice capture Udine, ending the independence of the Patria del Friuli.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Venice)

⚓ 1614 – The second parliament of James I of England ends after two months of deadlock and without passing any bills.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Addled_Parliament)

🚀 1776 – Richard Henry Lee presents the "Lee Resolution" to the Continental Congress. The motion is seconded by John Adams and will lead to the United States Declaration of Independence.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Henry_Lee)

💼 1991 – Mount Pinatubo erupts, generating an ash column 7 kilometres (4.3 mi) high.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mount_Pinatubo)

🎖️ 1938 – Second Sino-Japanese War: The Chinese Nationalist government creates the 1938 Yellow River flood to halt Japanese forces. Five hundred thousand to nine hundred thousand civilians are killed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Sino-Japanese_War)

📡 1962 – The Organisation Armée Secrète (OAS) sets fire to the University of Algiers library building, destroying about 500,000 books.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organisation_arm%C3%A9e_secr%C3%A8te)

🏙️ 1866 – One thousand eight hundred Fenian raiders are repelled back to the United States after looting and plundering the Saint-Armand and Frelighsburg areas of Canada East.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fenian_raids)

⚙️ 1955 – Lux Radio Theatre signs off the air permanently. The show launched in New York in 1934, and featured radio adaptations of Broadway shows and popular films.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lux_Radio_Theatre)

💥 1981 – The Israeli Air Force destroys Iraq's Osiraq nuclear reactor during Operation Opera.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Air_Force)

🕊️ 1965 – The Supreme Court of the United States hands down its decision in Griswold v. Connecticut, prohibiting the states from criminalizing the use of contraception by married couples.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States)

⚔️ 1948 – Edvard Beneš resigns as President of Czechoslovakia rather than signing the Ninth-of-May Constitution, making his nation a Communist state.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edvard_Bene%C5%A1)

🏰 1971 – The Alcohol, Tobacco, and Firearms Division of the U.S. Internal Revenue Service raids the home of Ken Ballew for illegal possession of hand grenades.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bureau_of_Alcohol%2C_Tobacco%2C_Firearms_and_Explosives)
