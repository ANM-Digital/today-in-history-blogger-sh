# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 70 – Siege of Jerusalem: Titus and his Roman legions breach the Second Wall of Jerusalem. Jewish defenders retreat to the First Wall. The Romans build a circumvallation, cutting down all trees within fifteen kilometres (9.3 mi).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_70)

🏰 1381 – Beginning of the Peasants' Revolt in England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peasants'_Revolt)

📜 1416 – The Council of Constance, called by Emperor Sigismund, a supporter of Antipope John XXIII, burns Jerome of Prague following a trial for heresy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Constance)

🛡️ 1510 – During the reign of the Zhengde Emperor, Ming dynasty rebel leader Zhu Zhifan is defeated by commander Qiu Yue, ending the Prince of Anhua rebellion.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhengde_Emperor)

⚓ 1631 – Publication of Gazette de France, the first French newspaper.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/La_Gazette_(France))

🚀 1723 – Johann Sebastian Bach assumes the office of Thomaskantor in Leipzig, presenting his first new cantata, Die Elenden sollen essen, BWV 75, in the St. Nicholas Church on the first Sunday after Trinity.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johann_Sebastian_Bach)

💼 1942 – World War II: One thousand British bombers launch a 90-minute attack on Cologne, Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bombing_of_Cologne_in_World_War_II)

🎖️ 1814 – The First Treaty of Paris is signed, returning the French frontiers to their 1792 extent, and restoring the House of Bourbon to power.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Paris_(1814))

📡 1883 – In New York City, 12 people are killed in a stampede on the recently opened Brooklyn Bridge.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brooklyn_Bridge)

🏙️ 1972 – In Ben Gurion Airport (at the time: Lod Airport), Israel, members of the Japanese Red Army carry out the Lod Airport massacre, killing 24 people and injuring 78 others.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ben_Gurion_Airport)

⚙️ 1854 – The Kansas–Nebraska Act becomes law, establishing the U.S. territories of Kansas and Nebraska.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kansas%E2%80%93Nebraska_Act)

💥 1943 – The Holocaust: Josef Mengele becomes chief medical officer of the Zigeunerfamilienlager (Romani family camp) at Auschwitz concentration camp.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Holocaust)

🕊️ 1866 – Bedrich Smetana's comic opera The Bartered Bride premieres in Prague.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bed%C5%99ich_Smetana)

⚔️ 1982 – Cold War: Spain joins NATO.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cold_War)

🏰 1963 – A protest against pro-Catholic discrimination during the Buddhist crisis is held outside South Vietnam's National Assembly, the first open demonstration during the eight-year presidency of Ngo Dinh Diem.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddhist_crisis)
