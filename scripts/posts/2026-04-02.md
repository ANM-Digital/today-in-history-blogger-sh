# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 1107 – Seljuq sultan Muhammad I Tapar begins with the siege of Shahdiz, a fortress of the Nizari Ismailis.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seljuk_Empire)

🏰 1285 – Election of Pope Honorius IV following the death of Pope Martin IV.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Honorius_IV)

📜 1513 – Having spotted land on March 27, Spanish explorer Juan Ponce de León comes ashore on what is now the U.S. state of Florida, landing somewhere between the modern city of St. Augustine and the mouth of the St. Johns River.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juan_Ponce_de_Le%C3%B3n)

🛡️ 1725 – J. S. Bach's cantata Bleib bei uns, denn es will Abend werden, BWV 6, is first performed in Leipzig on Easter Monday.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johann_Sebastian_Bach)

⚓ 1921 – The Autonomous Government of Khorasan, a military government encompassing the modern state of Iran, is established.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Autonomous_Government_of_Khorasan)

🚀 1956 – As the World Turns and The Edge of Night premiere on CBS. The two soaps become the first daytime dramas to debut in the 30-minute format.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/As_the_World_Turns)

💼 2012 – A mass shooting at Oikos University in California leaves seven people dead and three injured.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2012_Oikos_University_shooting)

🎖️ 2012 – UTair Flight 120 crashes after takeoff from Roshchino International Airport in Tyumen, Russia, killing 33 and injuring 10.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UTair_Flight_120)

📡 1992 – Forty-two civilians are massacred in the town of Bijeljina in Bosnia and Herzegovina.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bijeljina_massacre)

🏙️ 1991 – Rita Johnston becomes the first female Premier of a Canadian province when she succeeds William Vander Zalm (who had resigned) as Premier of British Columbia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rita_Johnston)

⚙️ 1801 – French Revolutionary Wars: In the Battle of Copenhagen a British Royal Navy squadron defeats a hastily assembled, smaller, mostly-volunteer Dano-Norwegian Navy at high cost, forcing Denmark out of the Second League of Armed Neutrality.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Revolutionary_Wars)

💥 1954 – A 19-month-old infant is swept up in the ocean tides at Hermosa Beach, California. Local photographer John L. Gaunt photographs the incident; 1955 Pulitzer winner "Tragedy by the Sea".. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hermosa_Beach%2C_California)

🕊️ 2004 – Islamist terrorists involved in the 11 March 2004 Madrid attacks attempt to bomb the Spanish high-speed train AVE near Madrid; the attack is thwarted.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamism)

⚔️ 1989 – Soviet leader Mikhail Gorbachev arrives in Havana, Cuba, to meet with Fidel Castro in an attempt to mend strained relations.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union)

🏰 2021 – At least 49 people are killed in a train derailment in Taiwan after a truck accidentally rolls onto the track.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2021_Hualien_train_derailment)
