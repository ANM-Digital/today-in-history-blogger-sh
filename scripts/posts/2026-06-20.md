# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 451 – Battle of Chalons: Flavius Aetius battles Attila the Hun. After the battle, which was inconclusive, Attila retreats.  Despite suffering catastrophic losses that led to the collapse of their alliance with the Goths and the destruction of much of the Roman army, the Romans nevertheless proclaimed victory despite the lack of any clear military success.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_the_Catalaunian_Plains)

🏰 1180 – First Battle of Uji, starting the Genpei War in Japan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Uji_(1180))

📜 1210 – Michael I Komnenos Doukas, the first ruler of the despotate of Epirus, signs a treaty with the Republic of Venice and becomes their vassal.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_I_Komnenos_Doukas)

🛡️ 1622 – The Battle of Höchst takes place during the  Thirty Years' War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_H%C3%B6chst)

⚓ 1756 – A British garrison is imprisoned in the Black Hole of Calcutta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Empire)

🚀 1900 – Baron Eduard Toll, leader of the Russian Polar Expedition of 1900, departs Saint Petersburg in Russia on the explorer ship Zarya, never to return.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eduard_von_Toll)

💼 1948 – The Deutsche Mark is introduced in Western Allied-occupied Germany. The Soviet Military Administration in Germany responded by imposing the Berlin Blockade four days later.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deutsche_Mark)

🎖️ 1990 – The 7.4 Mw Manjil–Rudbar earthquake affects northern Iran with a maximum Mercalli intensity of X (Extreme), killing 35,000–50,000, and injuring 60,000–105,000.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1990_Manjil%E2%80%93Rudbar_earthquake)

📡 1819 – The U.S. vessel SS Savannah arrives at Liverpool, United Kingdom. It is the first steam-propelled vessel to cross the Atlantic, although most of the journey is made under sail.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SS_Savannah)

🏙️ 1975 – The film Jaws is released in the United States, becoming the highest-grossing film of that time and starting the trend of films known as "summer blockbusters".. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jaws_(film))

⚙️ 1990 – Asteroid Eureka is discovered.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asteroid)

💥 2019 – Iran's Air Defense Forces shoot down an American surveillance drone over the Strait of Hormuz amid rising tensions between the two countries.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Republic_of_Iran_Air_Defense_Force)

🕊️ 1972 – Watergate scandal: An 18+1⁄2-minute gap appears in the tape recording of the conversations between U.S. President Richard Nixon and his advisers regarding the recent arrests of his operatives while breaking into the Watergate complex.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Watergate_scandal)

⚔️ 1991 – The German Bundestag votes to move seat of government from the former West German capital of Bonn to the present capital of Berlin.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bundestag)

🏰 1964 – A Curtiss C-46 Commando crashes in the Shengang District of Taiwan, killing 57 people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Curtiss_C-46_Commando)
