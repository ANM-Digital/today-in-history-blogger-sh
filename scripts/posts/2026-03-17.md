# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -45 – In his last victory, Julius Caesar defeats the Pompeian forces of Titus Labienus and Pompey the Younger in the Battle of Munda.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julius_Caesar)

🏰 180 – Commodus becomes sole emperor of the Roman Empire at the age of eighteen, following the death of his father, Marcus Aurelius.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commodus)

📜 455 – Petronius Maximus becomes, with support of the Roman Senate, emperor of the Western Roman Empire; he forces Licinia Eudoxia, the widow of his predecessor, Valentinian III, to marry him.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petronius_Maximus)

🛡️ 1337 – Edward the Black Prince is made Duke of Cornwall, the first duchy in England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_the_Black_Prince)

⚓ 1400 – Turko-Mongol emperor Timur sacks Damascus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turco-Mongol_tradition)

🚀 1776 – American Revolution: The British Army evacuates Boston, ending the Siege of Boston, after George Washington and Henry Knox place artillery in positions overlooking the city.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolution)

💼 1921 – The Second Polish Republic adopts the March Constitution.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Polish_Republic)

🎖️ 2003 – Leader of the House of Commons and Lord President of the Council, Robin Cook, resigns from the British Cabinet in disagreement with government plans for the 2003 invasion of Iraq.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leader_of_the_House_of_Commons)

📡 1988 – Eritrean War of Independence: The Nadew Command, an Ethiopian army corps in Eritrea, is attacked on three sides by military units of the Eritrean People's Liberation Front in the opening action of the Battle of Afabet.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eritrean_War_of_Independence)

🏙️ 1958 – The United States launches the first solar-powered satellite, which is also the first satellite to achieve a long-term orbit.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vanguard_1)

⚙️ 1891 – SS Utopia collides with HMS Anson in the Bay of Gibraltar and sinks, killing 562 of the 880 passengers on board.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SS_Utopia)

💥 1945 – The Ludendorff Bridge in Remagen, Germany, collapses, ten days after its capture.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ludendorff_Bridge)

🕊️ 1960 – Northwest Orient Airlines Flight 710 crashes in Tobin Township, Perry County, Indiana, killing 63.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northwest_Airlines_Flight_710)

⚔️ 1992 – Israeli Embassy attack in Buenos Aires: Car bomb attack kills 29 and injures 242.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1992_Buenos_Aires_Israeli_embassy_bombing)

🏰 1985 – Serial killer Richard Ramirez, aka the "Night Stalker", commits the first two murders in his Los Angeles murder spree.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Ramirez)
