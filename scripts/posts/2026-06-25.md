# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 524 – The Franks are defeated by the Burgundians in the Battle of Vézeronce.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Franks)

🏰 841 – In the Battle of Fontenay-en-Puisaye, forces led by Charles the Bald and Louis the German defeat the armies of Lothair I of Italy and Pepin II of Aquitaine.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Fontenoy_(841))

📜 1258 – War of Saint Sabas: In the Battle of Acre, the Venetians defeat a larger Genoese fleet sailing to relieve Acre.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_Saint_Sabas)

🛡️ 1357 – The English defeat a French fleet which aimed to bring relief to the besieged Calais in the battle of Crotoy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Crotoy_(1347))

⚓ 1401 – Schaffhausen massacre: 30 Jews were executed, following torture, after being accused of a blood libel in Schaffhausen (in present-day Switzerland).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schaffhausen_massacre)

🚀 1530 – At the Diet of Augsburg the Augsburg Confession is presented to the Holy Roman Emperor by the Lutheran princes and Electors of Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diet_of_Augsburg)

💼 1658 – Spanish forces fail to retake Jamaica at the Battle of Rio Nuevo during the Anglo-Spanish War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_Empire)

🎖️ 1741 – Maria Theresa is crowned Queen of Hungary.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maria_Theresa)

📡 1940 – World War II: The French armistice with Nazi Germany comes into effect.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

🏙️ 1996 – American rapper Jay-Z releases his debut album, Reasonable Doubt.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jay-Z)

⚙️ 2007 – PMTair Flight 241 crashes in the Dâmrei Mountains in Kampot Province, Cambodia, killing all 22 people on board.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PMTair_Flight_241)

💥 1998 – In Clinton v. City of New York, the United States Supreme Court decides that the Line Item Veto Act of 1996 is unconstitutional.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clinton_v._City_of_New_York)

🕊️ 1943 – The left-wing German Jewish exile Arthur Goldstein is murdered in Auschwitz.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arthur_Goldstein)

⚔️ 2022 – Russo-Ukrainian War: The Battle of Sievierodonetsk ends after weeks of heavy fighting with the Russian capture of the city, leading to the Battle of Lysychansk.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Ukrainian_war)

🏰 1935 – Colombia–Soviet Union relations are established.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombia%E2%80%93Russia_relations)
