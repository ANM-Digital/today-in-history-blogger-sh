# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 537 – Siege of Rome: King Vitiges attempts to assault the northern and eastern city walls, but is repulsed at the Praenestine Gate, known as the Vivarium, by the defenders under the Byzantine generals Bessas and Peranius.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Rome_(537%E2%80%93538))

🏰 630 – Emperor Heraclius returns the True Cross, one of the holiest Christian relics, to Jerusalem.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heraclius)

📜 717 – Battle of Vincy between Charles Martel and Ragenfrid.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Vincy)

🛡️ 1152 – Annulment of the marriage of King Louis VII of France and Queen Eleanor of Aquitaine.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_VII_of_France)

⚓ 1349 – Erfurt massacre: Outbreak of an antisemitic pogrom in Erfurt, Germany, during which hundreds to thousands of Jews were killed by Christians after being accused of causing the Black Death.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erfurt_massacre_(1349))

🚀 1556 – On the day of his execution in Oxford, former archbishop of Canterbury Thomas Cranmer deviates from the scripted sermon by renouncing the recantations he has made and adds, "And as for the pope, I refuse him, as Christ's enemy, and Antichrist with all his false doctrine.". [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oxford)

💼 1788 – A fire in New Orleans leaves most of the town in ruins.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_New_Orleans_Fire_(1788))

🎖️ 1994 – The United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change enters into force.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Framework_Convention_on_Climate_Change)

📡 1921 – The New Economic Policy is implemented by the Bolshevik Party in response to the economic failure as a result of war communism.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Economic_Policy)

🏙️ 1965 – Martin Luther King Jr. leads 3,200 people on the start of the third and finally successful civil rights march from Selma to Montgomery, Alabama.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martin_Luther_King_Jr.)

⚙️ 1943 – Wehrmacht officer Rudolf von Gersdorff plots to assassinate Adolf Hitler by using a suicide bomb, but the plan falls through; von Gersdorff is able to defuse the bomb in time and avoid suspicion.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wehrmacht)

💥 1970 – The first Earth Day proclamation is issued by Joseph Alioto, Mayor of San Francisco.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth_Day)

🕊️ 1986 – Debi Thomas becomes the first African American to win the World Figure Skating Championships.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Debi_Thomas)

⚔️ 1801 – The Battle of Alexandria is fought between British and French forces near the ruins of Nicopolis near Alexandria in Egypt.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Alexandria_(1801))

🏰 1919 – The Hungarian Soviet Republic is established becoming the first Communist government to be formed in Europe after the October Revolution in Russia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarian_Soviet_Republic)
