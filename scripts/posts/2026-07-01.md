# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 69 – Tiberius Julius Alexander orders his Roman legions in Alexandria to swear allegiance to Vespasian as Emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_69)

🏰 552 – Battle of Taginae: Byzantine forces under Narses defeat the Ostrogoths in Italy, and the Ostrogoth king, Totila, is mortally wounded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Taginae)

📜 1097 – Battle of Dorylaeum: Crusaders led by prince Bohemond of Taranto defeat a Seljuk army led by sultan Kilij Arslan I.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Dorylaeum_(1097))

🛡️ 1431 – The Battle of La Higueruela takes place in Granada, leading to a modest advance of the Kingdom of Castile during the Reconquista.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_La_Higueruela)

⚓ 1520 – Spanish conquistadors led by Hernán Cortés fight their way out of Tenochtitlan after nightfall.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conquistador)

🚀 1643 – First meeting of the Westminster Assembly, a council of theologians ("divines") and members of the Parliament of England appointed to restructure the Church of England, at Westminster Abbey in London.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Westminster_Assembly)

💼 1766 – François-Jean de la Barre, a young French nobleman, is tortured and beheaded before his body is burnt on a pyre along with a copy of Voltaire's Dictionnaire philosophique nailed to his torso for the crime of not saluting a Roman Catholic religious procession in Abbeville, France.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fran%C3%A7ois-Jean_de_la_Barre)

🎖️ 1957 – The International Geophysical Year begins.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Geophysical_Year)

📡 2008 – Riots erupt in Mongolia in response to allegations of fraud surrounding the 2008 legislative elections.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2008_riot_in_Mongolia)

🏙️ 2002 – Bashkirian Airlines Flight 2937, a Tupolev Tu-154, and DHL Flight 611, a Boeing 757, collide in mid-air over Überlingen, southern Germany, killing all 71 on board both planes.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tupolev_Tu-154)

⚙️ 1958 – Flooding of Canada's Saint Lawrence Seaway begins.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/St._Lawrence_Seaway)

💥 1980 – "O Canada" officially becomes the national anthem of Canada.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/O_Canada)

🕊️ 1942 – The Australian Federal Government becomes the sole collector of income tax in Australia as State Income Tax is abolished.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_Government)

⚔️ 1987 – The American radio station WFAN in New York City is launched as the world's first all-sports radio station.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WFAN_(AM))

🏰 1862 – Princess Alice of the United Kingdom, second daughter of Queen Victoria, marries Prince Louis of Hesse, the future Louis IV, Grand Duke of Hesse.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Princess_Alice_of_the_United_Kingdom)
