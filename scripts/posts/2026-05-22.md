⚔️ 192 – Dong Zhuo is assassinated by his adopted son Lü Bu.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dong_Zhuo)

🏰 760 – Fourteenth recorded perihelion passage of Halley's Comet.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Halley's_Comet)

🛡️ 1176 – The Hashshashin (Assassins) attempt to assassinate Saladin near Aleppo.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Order_of_Assassins)

💼 1455 – Start of the Wars of the Roses: At the First Battle of St Albans, Richard, Duke of York, defeats and captures King Henry VI of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wars_of_the_Roses)

🎖️ 1520 – The massacre at the festival of Tóxcatl takes place during the Fall of Tenochtitlan, resulting in turning the Aztecs against the Spanish.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massacre_in_the_Great_Temple_of_Tenochtitlan)

📡 1629 – Holy Roman Emperor Ferdinand II and Danish King Christian IV sign the Treaty of Lübeck ending Danish intervention in the Thirty Years' War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Roman_Emperor)

🏙️ 1762 – Sweden and Prussia sign the Treaty of Hamburg.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prussia)

⚙️ 1967 – Egypt closes the Straits of Tiran to Israeli shipping.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Straits_of_Tiran)

💥 1957 – South Africa's government approves of racial separation in universities.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apartheid)

🕊️ 1809 – On the second and last day of the Battle of Aspern-Essling (near Vienna, Austria), Napoleon I is defeated in a major battle for the first time in his career, and repelled by an enemy army for the first time in a decade.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Aspern%E2%80%93Essling)

⚔️ 1804 – The Lewis and Clark Expedition officially begins as the Corps of Discovery departs from St. Charles, Missouri.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lewis_and_Clark_Expedition)
