# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 17 – Germanicus celebrates a triumph in Rome for his victories over the Cherusci, Chatti, and other German tribes west of the Elbe.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_17)

🏰 451 – Battle of Avarayr between Armenian rebels and the Sasanian Empire takes place. The Sasanids defeat the Armenians militarily but guarantee them freedom to openly practice Christianity.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Avarayr)

📜 866 – Basil I is crowned as co-emperor of the Byzantine Empire by Michael III.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Basil_I)

🛡️ 946 – England is left temporarily without a monarch after the death of King Edmund I in a street fight, resulting in Edmund's brother Eadred assuming the throne for the minority of Edmund's two sons.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_England)

⚓ 1135 – Alfonso VII of León and Castile is crowned in León Cathedral as Imperator totius Hispaniae (Emperor of all of Spain).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfonso_VII_of_Le%C3%B3n_and_Castile)

🚀 1293 – An earthquake strikes Kamakura, Kanagawa, Japan, killing about 23,000.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1293_Kamakura_earthquake)

💼 1328 – William of Ockham, the Franciscan Minister-General Michael of Cesena, and two other Franciscan leaders secretly leave Avignon, fearing a death sentence from Pope John XXII.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_of_Ockham)

🎖️ 1538 – Geneva expels John Calvin and his followers from the city. Calvin lives in exile in Strasbourg for the next three years.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geneva)

📡 1637 – Pequot War: A combined English and Mohegan force under John Mason attacks a village in Connecticut, massacring approximately 500 Pequots.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pequot_War)

🏙️ 1736 – The Battle of Ackia is fought near the present site of Tupelo, Mississippi. British and Chickasaw soldiers repel a French and Choctaw attack on the then-Chickasaw village of Ackia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chickasaw_Campaign_of_1736)

⚙️ 2025 – 65 people are injured when a car rams into a crowd on Water Street, near Liverpool F.C.'s Premier League trophy parade.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Liverpool_parade_attack)

💥 1969 – Apollo program: Apollo 10 returns to Earth after a successful eight-day test of all the components needed for the forthcoming first crewed Moon landing.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_program)

🕊️ 1940 – World War II: The Siege of Calais ends with the surrender of the British and French garrison.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Calais_(1940))

⚔️ 1998 – The first "National Sorry Day" is held in Australia. Reconciliation events are held nationally, and attended by over a million people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Sorry_Day)

🏰 1822 – At least 113 people die in the Grue Church fire, the biggest fire disaster in Norway's history.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grue_Church_fire)
