# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 1139 – Pope Innocent II issues the bull Omne datum optimum in which he endorses the Knights Templar and approves the Templar Rule.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Innocent_II)

🏰 1430 – The Ottoman Empire under Murad II captures Thessalonica from the Republic of Venice.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Empire)

📜 1549 – The city of Salvador, Bahia, the first capital of Brazil, is founded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salvador%2C_Bahia)

🛡️ 1632 – Treaty of Saint-Germain is signed returning Quebec to French control after the English had seized it in 1629.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Saint-Germain-en-Laye_(1632))

⚓ 1792 – King Gustav III of Sweden dies after being shot in the back at a midnight masquerade ball at Stockholm's Royal Opera 13 days earlier.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gustav_III)

🚀 1990 – The Czechoslovak parliament is unable to reach an agreement on what to call the country after the fall of Communism, sparking the so-called Hyphen War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Czechoslovakia)

💼 1999 – A magnitude 6.8 earthquake in India strikes the Chamoli district in Uttar Pradesh, killing 103.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1999_Chamoli_earthquake)

🎖️ 2013 – At least 36 people are killed when a 16-floor building collapses in the commercial capital Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2013_Dar_es_Salaam_building_collapse)

📡 1882 – The Knights of Columbus is established.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Knights_of_Columbus)

🏙️ 2002 – In reaction to the Passover massacre two days prior, Israel launches Operation Defensive Shield against Palestinian militants, its largest military operation in the West Bank since the 1967 Six-Day War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Passover_massacre)

⚙️ 2016 – A United States Air Force F-16 crashes during takeoff from Bagram Airfield in Afghanistan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Air_Force)

💥 1982 – The Canada Act 1982 receives the Royal Assent from Queen Elizabeth II, setting the stage for the Queen of Canada to proclaim the Constitution Act, 1982.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada_Act_1982)

🕊️ 2021 – The ship Ever Given was dislodged from the Suez Canal.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ever_Given)

⚔️ 1974 – Terracotta Army was discovered in Shaanxi province, China.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terracotta_Army)

🏰 1974 – NASA's Mariner 10 becomes the first space probe to fly by Mercury.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NASA)
