# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -597 – The first siege of Jerusalem by the Neo-Babylonian Empire ends with the city surrendering to king Nebuchadnezzar II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Jerusalem_(597_BC))

🏰 445 – Western Roman Emperor Valentinian III is assassinated  while practising on the Campus Martius on behalf of Petronius Maximus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_emperor)

📜 1190 – Massacre of Jews at Clifford's Tower, York.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/York_Castle)

🛡️ 1244 – Over 200 Cathars who refuse to recant are burnt to death after the Fall of Montségur.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catharism)

⚓ 1355 – Amidst the Red Turban Rebellions, Han Lin'er, a claimed descendant of Emperor Huizong of Song, is proclaimed emperor of the restored Song dynasty in Bozhou.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Turban_Rebellions)

🚀 1621 – Samoset, an Abenaki, visits the settlers of Plymouth Colony and greets them, "Welcome, Englishmen! My name is Samoset.". [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samoset)

💼 1792 – King Gustav III of Sweden is shot; he dies on March 29.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gustav_III)

🎖️ 1968 – Vietnam War: My Lai massacre occurs; between 347 and 500 Vietnamese villagers are killed by American troops.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam_War)

📡 1988 – Iran–Contra affair: Lieutenant Colonel Oliver North and Vice Admiral John Poindexter are indicted on charges of conspiracy to defraud the United States.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran%E2%80%93Contra_affair)

🏙️ 1995 – Mississippi formally ratifies the Thirteenth Amendment to the United States Constitution, becoming the last state to approve the abolition of slavery. The Thirteenth Amendment was officially ratified in 1865.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi)

⚙️ 1945 – Ninety percent of Würzburg, Germany is destroyed in only 20 minutes by British bombers, resulting in at least 4,000 deaths.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bombing_of_W%C3%BCrzburg_in_World_War_II)

💥 1924 – In accordance with the Treaty of Rome, Fiume becomes annexed as part of Italy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Rome_(1924))

🕊️ 1985 – Associated Press newsman Terry Anderson is taken hostage in Beirut; he is not released until December 1991.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Associated_Press)

⚔️ 1918 – Finnish Civil War: Battle of Länkipohja is infamous for its bloody aftermath as the Whites execute 70–100 capitulated Reds.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finnish_Civil_War)

🏰 2005 – Israel officially hands over Jericho to Palestinian control.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel)
