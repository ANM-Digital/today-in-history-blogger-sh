# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 625 – The Muslim army under Muhammad suffers a defeat against the Quraysh in the battle of Uhud.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad)

🏰 1400 – The Trần dynasty of Vietnam is deposed, after one hundred and seventy-five years of rule, by Hồ Quý Ly, a court official.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tr%E1%BA%A7n_dynasty)

📜 1540 – Waltham Abbey is surrendered to King Henry VIII of England, the last religious community to be closed during the Dissolution of the Monasteries.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waltham_Abbey_Church)

🛡️ 1775 – American Revolutionary War: Patrick Henry delivers his speech – "Give me liberty or give me death!" – at St. John's Episcopal Church, Richmond, Virginia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War)

⚓ 1940 – The Lahore Resolution (Qarardad-e-Pakistan or Qarardad-e-Lahore) is put forward at the Annual General Convention of the All-India Muslim League.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lahore_Resolution)

🚀 1956 – Pakistan becomes the first Islamic republic in the world. This date is now celebrated as Republic Day in Pakistan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan)

💼 1980 – Archbishop Óscar Romero of El Salvador gives his famous speech appealing to men of the El Salvadoran armed forces to stop killing the Salvadorans.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%93scar_Romero)

🎖️ 1806 – After traveling through the Louisiana Purchase and reaching the Pacific Ocean, explorers Lewis and Clark and their Corps of Discovery begin their arduous journey home.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louisiana_Purchase)

📡 2010 – The Affordable Care Act becomes law in the United States.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Affordable_Care_Act)

🏙️ 1918 – First World War: On the third day of the German Spring Offensive, the 10th Battalion of the Royal West Kent Regiment is annihilated with many of the men becoming prisoners of war.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I)

⚙️ 2019 – The Kazakh capital of Astana is renamed to Nur-Sultan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazakhstan)

💥 1991 – The Revolutionary United Front, with support from the special forces of Charles Taylor's National Patriotic Front of Liberia, invades Sierra Leone in an attempt to overthrow Joseph Saidu Momoh, sparking the 11-year Sierra Leone Civil War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Revolutionary_United_Front)

🕊️ 1931 – Bhagat Singh, Shivaram Rajguru and Sukhdev Thapar are hanged for the killing of a deputy superintendent of police during the Indian independence movement.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bhagat_Singh)

⚔️ 1933 – The Reichstag passes the Enabling Act of 1933, making Adolf Hitler dictator of Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reichstag_(Weimar_Republic))

🏰 1988 – Angolan and Cuban forces defeat South Africa in the Battle of Cuito Cuanavale.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Cuito_Cuanavale)
