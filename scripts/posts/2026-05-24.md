# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 919 – The nobles of Franconia and Saxony elect Henry the Fowler at the Imperial Diet in Fritzlar as king of the East Frankish Kingdom.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Franconia)

🏰 1218 – The Fifth Crusade leaves Acre for Egypt.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fifth_Crusade)

📜 1487 – The ten-year-old Lambert Simnel is crowned in Christ Church Cathedral, Dublin, Ireland, with the name of Edward VI in a bid to threaten King Henry VII's reign.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lambert_Simnel)

🛡️ 1567 – Erik XIV of Sweden and his guards murder five incarcerated Swedish nobles.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erik_XIV)

⚓ 1607 – Jamestown, the first permanent English colony in North America, is founded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jamestown%2C_Virginia)

🚀 1738 – John Wesley is converted, essentially launching the Methodist movement; the day is celebrated annually by Methodists as Aldersgate Day and a church service is generally held on the preceding Sunday.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Wesley)

💼 1976 – The Judgment of Paris takes place in France, launching California as a worldwide force in the production of quality wine.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judgment_of_Paris_(wine))

🎖️ 1930 – Amy Johnson lands in Darwin, Northern Territory, becoming the first woman to fly solo from England to Australia (she left on May 5 for the 11,000 mile flight).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amy_Johnson)

📡 1993 – Roman Catholic Cardinal Juan Jesús Posadas Ocampo and five other people are assassinated in a shootout at Miguel Hidalgo y Costilla Guadalajara International Airport in Mexico.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juan_Jes%C3%BAs_Posadas_Ocampo)

🏙️ 1988 – Section 28 of the United Kingdom's Local Government Act 1988, a controversial amendment stating that a local authority cannot intentionally promote homosexuality, is enacted.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Section_28)

⚙️ 1967 – Egypt imposes a blockade and siege of the Red Sea coast of Israel.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt)

💥 1956 – The first Eurovision Song Contest is held in Lugano, Switzerland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eurovision_Song_Contest)

🕊️ 1941 – World War II: Battle of the Atlantic: In the Battle of the Denmark Strait, the German battleship Bismarck sinks the pride of the Royal Navy, HMS Hood, killing all but three crewmen.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

⚔️ 1993 – Eritrea gains its independence from Ethiopia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eritrea)

🏰 1948 – Arab–Israeli War: Egypt captures the Israeli kibbutz of Yad Mordechai, but the five-day effort gives Israeli forces time to prepare enough to stop the Egyptian advance a week later.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1948_Arab%E2%80%93Israeli_War)
