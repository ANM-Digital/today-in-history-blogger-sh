# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 363 – Jovian is proclaimed Roman emperor following the death of emperor Julian in the previous night.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jovian_(emperor))

🏰 678 – Pope Agatho is consecrated following the death of pope Donus two month prior.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Agatho)

📜 1499 – Amerigo Vespucci sights what is now Amapá State in Brazil.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amerigo_Vespucci)

🛡️ 1556 – The thirteen Stratford Martyrs are burned at the stake near London for their Protestant beliefs.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stratford_Martyrs)

⚓ 1743 – In the Battle of Dettingen, George II becomes the last reigning British monarch to participate in a battle.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Dettingen)

🚀 1950 – The United States decides to send troops to fight in the Korean War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Korean_War)

💼 1895 – The inaugural run of the Baltimore and Ohio Railroad's Royal Blue from Washington, D.C., to New York City, the first U.S. passenger train to use electric locomotives.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baltimore_and_Ohio_Railroad)

🎖️ 2015 – Formosa Fun Coast fire: A dust fire occurs at a recreational water park in Taiwan, killing 15 people and injuring 497 others, 199 critically.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_New_Taipei_water_park_fire)

📡 1995 – Space Shuttle Atlantis launches on STS-71, the first space shuttle mission to dock with the Russian space station Mir.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Atlantis)

🏙️ 1954 – The FIFA World Cup quarterfinal match between Hungary and Brazil, highly anticipated to be exciting, instead turns violent, with three players ejected and further fighting continuing after the game.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1954_FIFA_World_Cup)

⚙️ 1928 – The Rovaniemi township decree is promulgated, as a result of which Rovaniemi secedes from the old rural municipality as its own market town on January 1, 1929.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rovaniemi)

💥 1914 – The Illinois Monument is dedicated at Cheatham Hill in what is now the Kennesaw Mountain National Battlefield Park.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illinois_Monument)

🕊️ 1954 – The Obninsk Nuclear Power Plant, the Soviet Union's first nuclear power station, opens in Obninsk, near Moscow.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Obninsk_Nuclear_Power_Plant)

⚔️ 1982 – Space Shuttle Columbia launched from the Kennedy Space Center on the final research and development flight mission, STS-4.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Columbia)

🏰 1806 – British forces take Buenos Aires during the first of the British invasions of the River Plate.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buenos_Aires)
