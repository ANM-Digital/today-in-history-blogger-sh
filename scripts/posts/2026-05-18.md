# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 332 – Emperor Constantine the Great announces free distributions of food to the citizens in Constantinople.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantine_the_Great)

🏰 872 – Louis II of Italy is crowned for the second time as Holy Roman Emperor at Rome, at the age of 47. His first coronation was 28 years earlier, in 844, during the reign of his father Lothair I.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_II_of_Italy)

📜 1096 – First Crusade: Around 800 Jews are massacred in Worms, Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Crusade)

🛡️ 1152 – The future Henry II of England marries Eleanor of Aquitaine. He would become king two years later, after the death of his cousin once removed King Stephen of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_II_of_England)

⚓ 1268 – The Principality of Antioch, a crusader state, falls to the Mamluk Sultan Baibars in the Siege of Antioch.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Principality_of_Antioch)

🚀 1302 – Bruges Matins, the nocturnal massacre of the French garrison in Bruges by members of the local Flemish militia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matins_of_Bruges)

💼 1499 – Alonso de Ojeda sets sail from Cádiz on his voyage to what is now Venezuela.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alonso_de_Ojeda)

🎖️ 1565 – The Great Siege of Malta begins, in which Ottoman forces attempt and fail to conquer Malta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Siege_of_Malta)

📡 1631 – In Dorchester, Massachusetts, John Winthrop takes the oath of office and becomes the first Governor of Massachusetts.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dorchester%2C_Boston)

🏙️ 1756 – The Seven Years' War begins when Great Britain declares war on France.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seven_Years'_War)

⚙️ 1900 – The United Kingdom proclaims a protectorate over Tonga.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Protectorate)

💥 1977 – Likud party wins the 1977 Israeli legislative election, with Menachem Begin, its founder, as the sixth Prime Minister of Israel.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Likud)

🕊️ 1933 – New Deal: President Franklin D. Roosevelt signs an act creating the Tennessee Valley Authority.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Deal)

⚔️ 1990 – In France, a modified TGV train achieves a new rail world speed record of 515.3 km/h (320.2 mph).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TGV)

🏰 2005 – A second photo from the Hubble Space Telescope confirms that Pluto has two additional moons, Nix and Hydra.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hubble_Space_Telescope)
