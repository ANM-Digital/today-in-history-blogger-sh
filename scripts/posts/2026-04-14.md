# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -43 – Legions loyal to the Roman Senate, commanded by Gaius Pansa, defeat the forces of Mark Antony in the Battle of Forum Gallorum.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Senate)

🏰 69 – Vitellius, commanding Rhine-based armies, defeats Roman emperor Otho in the First Battle of Bedriacum to take power over Rome.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_69)

📜 966 – Following his marriage to the Christian Doubravka of Bohemia, the pagan ruler of the Polans, Mieszko I, converts to Christianity, an event considered to be the founding of the Polish state.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doubravka_of_Bohemia)

🛡️ 1205 – Combined Bulgarian and Cuman army under Kalojan ambushes and defeats forces of the Latin Empire of Constantinople in the Battle of Adrianople.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kaloyan_of_Bulgaria)

⚓ 1395 – Tokhtamysh–Timur war: At the Battle of the Terek River, Timur defeats the army of the Golden Horde, beginning the khanate's permanent military decline.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tokhtamysh%E2%80%93Timur_war)

🚀 1471 – In England, the Yorkists under Edward IV defeat the Lancastrians under the Earl of Warwick at the Battle of Barnet; the Earl is killed and Edward resumes the throne.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/House_of_York)

💼 1561 – A celestial phenomenon is reported over Nuremberg, described as an aerial battle.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1561_celestial_phenomenon_over_Nuremberg)

🎖️ 1639 – Thirty Years' War: Forces of the Holy Roman Empire and Electorate of Saxony are defeated by the Swedes at the Battle of Chemnitz, ending the military effectiveness of the Saxon army for the rest of the war and allowing the Swedes to advance into Bohemia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thirty_Years'_War)

📡 1775 – The Society for the Relief of Free Negroes Unlawfully Held in Bondage, the first abolition society in North America, is organized in Philadelphia by Benjamin Franklin and Benjamin Rush.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pennsylvania_Abolition_Society)

🏙️ 1944 – Bombay explosion: A massive explosion in Bombay harbor kills 300 and causes economic damage valued at 20 million pounds.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1944_Bombay_explosion)

⚙️ 1909 – Muslims in the Ottoman Empire begin a massacre of Armenians in Adana.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Empire)

💥 1945 – Razing of Friesoythe: The 4th Canadian (Armoured) Division deliberately destroys the German town of Friesoythe on the orders of Major General Christopher Vokes.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Razing_of_Friesoythe)

🕊️ 1997 – Pai Hsiao-yen, daughter of Taiwanese artiste Pai Bing-bing is kidnapped on her way to school, preceding her murder.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Murder_of_Pai_Hsiao-yen)

⚔️ 2024 – Flooding in the Persian Gulf starts, killing 19 in Oman.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_Persian_Gulf_floods)

🏰 1986 – The heaviest hailstones ever recorded, each weighing 1 kilogram (2.2 lb), fall on the Gopalganj district of Bangladesh, killing 92.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hail)
