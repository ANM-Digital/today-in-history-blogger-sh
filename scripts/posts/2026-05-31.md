# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 455 – Emperor Petronius Maximus is stoned to death by an angry mob while fleeing Rome.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petronius_Maximus)

🏰 1215 – Zhongdu (now Beijing), then under the control of the Jurchen ruler Emperor Xuanzong of Jin, is captured by the Mongols under Genghis Khan, ending the Battle of Zhongdu.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhongdu)

📜 1578 – King Henry III lays the first stone of the Pont Neuf (New Bridge), the oldest bridge of Paris, France.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_III_of_France)

🛡️ 1610 – The pageant London's Love to Prince Henry on the River Thames celebrates the creation of Prince Henry as Prince of Wales.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London's_Love_to_Prince_Henry)

⚓ 1775 – American Revolution: The Mecklenburg Resolves are adopted in the Province of North Carolina.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolution)

🚀 1947 – Ferenc Nagy, the democratically elected Prime Minister of Hungary, resigns from office after blackmail from the Hungarian Communist Party accusing him of being part of a plot against the state. This grants the Communists effective control of the Hungarian government.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferenc_Nagy)

💼 1955 – The U.S. Supreme Court expands on its Brown v. Board of Education decision by ordering district courts and school districts to enforce educational desegregation "at all deliberate speed.". [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States)

🎖️ 1961 – The South African Constitution of 1961 becomes effective, thus creating the Republic of South Africa, which remains outside the Commonwealth of Nations until 1 June 1994, when South Africa is returned to Commonwealth membership.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_African_Constitution_of_1961)

📡 1941 – Anglo-Iraqi War: The United Kingdom completes the re-occupation of Iraq and returns 'Abd al-Ilah to power as regent for Faisal II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anglo-Iraqi_War)

🏙️ 1911 – The President of Mexico Porfirio Díaz flees the country during the Mexican Revolution.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Mexico)

⚙️ 1985 – United States–Canada tornado outbreak: Forty-one tornadoes hit Ohio, Pennsylvania, New York, and Ontario, leaving 76 dead.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1985_United_States%E2%80%93Canada_tornado_outbreak)

💥 1971 – In accordance with the Uniform Monday Holiday Act passed by the U.S. Congress in 1968, observation of Memorial Day occurs on the last Monday in May for the first time, rather than on the traditional Memorial Day of May 30.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uniform_Monday_Holiday_Act)

🕊️ 1847 – Qajar Iran and the Ottoman Empire determine their international boundary in the second treaty of Erzurum.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qajar_Iran)

⚔️ 2013 – The asteroid 1998 QE2 and its moon make their closest approach to Earth for the next two centuries.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asteroid)

🏰 2017 – A car bomb explodes in a crowded intersection in Kabul near the German embassy during rush hour, killing over 90 and injuring 463.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/May_2017_Kabul_bombing)
