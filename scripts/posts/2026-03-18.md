# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 37 – Roman Senate annuls Tiberius' will and proclaims Gaius Julius Caesar Augustus Germanicus (aka Caligula = Little Boots) emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_37)

🏰 417 – Pope Zosimus is elected following the death of pope Innocent I.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Zosimus)

📜 1068 – An earthquake in the Levant and the Arabian Peninsula leaves up to 20,000 dead.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1068_Near_East_earthquakes)

🛡️ 1229 – Frederick II, Holy Roman Emperor, declares himself King of Jerusalem in the Sixth Crusade.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederick_II%2C_Holy_Roman_Emperor)

⚓ 1314 – Jacques de Molay, the 23rd and final Grand Master of the Knights Templar, is burned at the stake.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacques_de_Molay)

🚀 1438 – Albert II of Habsburg becomes King of the Romans.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_II_of_Germany)

💼 1571 – Valletta is made the capital city of Malta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Valletta)

🎖️ 1608 – Susenyos is formally crowned Emperor of Ethiopia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Susenyos_I)

📡 1741 – New York governor George Clarke's complex at Fort George is burned in an arson attack, starting the New York Conspiracy of 1741.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Clarke_(governor))

🏙️ 1990 – Germans in the German Democratic Republic vote in the first democratic elections in the former communist dictatorship.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Germany)

⚙️ 2015 – The Bardo National Museum in Tunisia is attacked by gunmen. Twenty-four people, almost all tourists, are killed, and at least 50 other people are wounded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bardo_National_Museum_(Tunis))

💥 1970 – Lon Nol ousts Prince Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lon_Nol)

🕊️ 1925 – The 1925 Tri-State tornado hits the Midwestern states of Missouri, Illinois, and Indiana, killing 695 people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1925_Tri-State_tornado)

⚔️ 1942 – The War Relocation Authority is established in the United States to take Japanese Americans into custody.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_Relocation_Authority)

🏰 2014 – The parliaments of Russia and Crimea sign an accession treaty.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crimea)
