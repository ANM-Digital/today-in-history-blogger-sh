# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 260 – Sima Zhao's regicide of Cao Mao: The figurehead Wei emperor Cao Mao personally leads an attempt to oust his regent, Sima Zhao; the attempted coup is crushed and the emperor killed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sima_Zhao's_regicide_of_Cao_Mao)

🏰 455 – Sack of Rome: Vandals enter Rome, and plunder the city for two weeks.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sack_of_Rome_(455))

📜 575 – Pope Benedict I is consecrated after obtaining imperial approval from Constantinople.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Benedict_I)

🛡️ 1098 – First Crusade: The first Siege of Antioch ends as Crusader forces take the city; the second siege began five days later.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Crusade)

⚓ 1259 – Wedding of Manfred, King of Sicily, and Byzantine princess Helena Angelina Doukaina.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manfred%2C_King_of_Sicily)

🚀 1608 – The Colony of Virginia gets a charter, extending borders from "sea to sea".. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colony_of_Virginia)

💼 1763 – Pontiac's Rebellion: At what is now Mackinaw City, Michigan, Chippewas capture Fort Michilimackinac by diverting the garrison's attention with a game of lacrosse, then chasing a ball into the fort.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pontiac's_War)

🎖️ 1953 – The coronation of Queen Elizabeth II at Westminster Abbey becomes the first British coronation, and one of the first major international events, to be televised.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coronation_of_Elizabeth_II)

📡 1967 – Luis Monge is executed in Colorado's gas chamber, in the last pre-Furman execution in the United States.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luis_Monge_(mass_murderer))

🏙️ 1941 – World War II: German paratroopers murder Greek civilians in the villages of Kondomari and Alikianos.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

⚙️ 1910 – Charles Rolls, a co-founder of Rolls-Royce Limited, becomes the first man to make a non-stop double crossing of the English Channel by plane.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Rolls)

💥 1848 – The Slavic Congress opens in Prague.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prague_Slavic_Congress%2C_1848)

🕊️ 1866 – The Fenians defeat Canadian forces at Ridgeway and Fort Erie, but the raids end soon after.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fenian_Brotherhood)

⚔️ 1946 – Birth of the Italian Republic: In a referendum, Italians vote to turn Italy from a monarchy into a Republic. After the referendum, King Umberto II of Italy is exiled.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1946_Italian_institutional_referendum)

🏰 1966 – Surveyor program: Surveyor 1 lands in Oceanus Procellarum on the Moon, becoming the first U.S. spacecraft to soft-land on another world.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Surveyor_program)
