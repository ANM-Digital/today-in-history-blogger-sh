# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 293 – Roman Emperors Diocletian and Maximian appoint Galerius as Caesar to Diocletian, beginning the period of four rulers known as the Tetrarchy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diocletian)

🏰 878 – Syracuse, Sicily, is captured by the Muslim Aghlabids after a nine-month siege.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syracuse%2C_Sicily)

📜 996 – Sixteen-year-old Otto III is crowned Holy Roman Emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Otto_III%2C_Holy_Roman_Emperor)

🛡️ 1294 – Coronation of Michael IX Palaiologos as Byzantine Emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_IX_Palaiologos)

⚓ 1347 – Coronation of John VI Kantakouzenos as Byzantine Emperor by patriarch Isidore I of Constantinople at the Church of St. Mary of Blachernae.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_VI_Kantakouzenos)

🚀 1403 – Henry III of Castile sends Ruy González de Clavijo as ambassador to Timur to discuss the possibility of an alliance between Timur and Castile against the Ottoman Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_III_of_Castile)

💼 1554 – Queen Mary I grants a royal charter to Derby School, as a grammar school for boys in Derby, England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary_I)

🎖️ 1659 – In the Concert of The Hague, the Dutch Republic, the Commonwealth of England and the Kingdom of France set out their views on how the Second Northern War should end.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Concert_of_The_Hague_(1659))

📡 1703 – Daniel Defoe is imprisoned on charges of seditious libel.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_Defoe)

🏙️ 2010 – JAXA, the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency, launches the solar-sail spacecraft IKAROS aboard an H-IIA rocket. The vessel would make a Venus flyby late in the year.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/JAXA)

⚙️ 1917 – The Great Atlanta fire of 1917 causes $5.5 million in damages, destroying some 300 acres including 2,000 homes, businesses and churches, displacing about 10,000 people but leading to only one fatality (due to heart attack).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Atlanta_fire_of_1917)

💥 1894 – The Manchester Ship Canal in the United Kingdom is officially opened by Queen Victoria, who later knights its designer Sir Edward Leader Williams.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manchester_Ship_Canal)

🕊️ 1998 – In Miami, five abortion clinics are attacked by a butyric acid attacker.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miami)

⚔️ 2024 – A stabbing spree on the Green line of the Taichung MRT injures four people, including the perpetrator.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_Taichung_MRT_attack)

🏰 2012 – A suicide bombing kills more than 120 people in Sanaa, Yemen.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2012_Unity_Day_parade_rehearsal_bombing)
