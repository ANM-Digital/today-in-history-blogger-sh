# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 639 – Ashina Jiesheshuai and his tribesmen assaulted Emperor Taizong at Jiucheng Palace.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ashina_Jiesheshuai)

🏰 715 – Pope Gregory II is elected.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Gregory_II)

📜 934 – The Byzantine Empire reconquers Melitene under the leadership of John Kourkouas.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire)

🛡️ 1051 – Henry I of France marries the Rus' princess, Anne of Kiev.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_I_of_France)

⚓ 1445 – John II of Castile defeats the Infantes of Aragon at the First Battle of Olmedo.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_II_of_Castile)

🚀 1535 – French explorer Jacques Cartier sets sail on his second voyage to North America with three ships, 110 men, and Chief Donnacona's two sons (whom Cartier had kidnapped during his first voyage).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacques_Cartier)

💼 1643 – Thirty Years' War: French forces under the duc d'Enghien decisively defeat Spanish forces at the Battle of Rocroi, marking the symbolic end of Spain as a dominant land power.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thirty_Years'_War)

🎖️ 1743 – Jean-Pierre Christin developed the centigrade temperature scale.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-Pierre_Christin)

📡 2007 – President of Romania Traian Băsescu survives an impeachment referendum and returns to office from suspension.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Romania)

🏙️ 1993 – SAM Colombia Flight 501 crashes on approach to José María Córdova International Airport in Medellín, Colombia, killing 132.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SAM_Colombia_Flight_501)

⚙️ 1942 – World War II: In the aftermath of the Battle of the Coral Sea, Task Force 16 heads to Pearl Harbor for repairs.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

💥 2012 – Three gas cylinder bombs explode in front of a vocational school in the Italian city of Brindisi, killing one person and injuring five others.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brindisi_school_bombing)

🕊️ 2018 – The wedding of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle is held at St George's Chapel, Windsor, with an estimated global audience of 1.9 billion.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wedding_of_Prince_Harry_and_Meghan_Markle)

⚔️ 2012 – A car bomb explodes near a military complex in the Syrian city of Deir ez-Zor, killing nine people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2012_Deir_ez-Zor_bombing)

🏰 1917 – The Norwegian football club Rosenborg BK is founded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rosenborg_BK)
