# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -404 – Admiral Lysander and King Pausanias of Sparta blockade Athens and bring the Peloponnesian War to a successful conclusion.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lysander)

🏰 775 – The Battle of Bagrevand puts an end to an Armenian rebellion against the Abbasid Caliphate. Muslim control over the South Caucasus is solidified and its Islamization begins, while several major Armenian nakharar families lose power and their remnants flee to the Byzantine Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Bagrevand)

📜 1134 – The name Zagreb was mentioned for the first time in the Felician Charter relating to the establishment of the Zagreb Bishopric around 1094.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zagreb)

🛡️ 1464 – A Yorkist army under the Baron Montagu defeats a Lancastrian army under the Duke of Somerset in the battle of Hedgeley Moor during the Wars of the Roses.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/House_of_York)

⚓ 1607 – Eighty Years' War: The Dutch fleet destroys the anchored Spanish fleet at Gibraltar.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eighty_Years'_War)

🚀 1707 – A coalition of Britain, the Netherlands and Portugal is defeated by a Franco-Spanish army at Almansa (Spain) in the War of the Spanish Succession.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Almansa)

💼 1945 – United Nations Conference on International Organization: Founding negotiations for the United Nations begin in San Francisco.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Conference_on_International_Organization)

🎖️ 1862 – American Civil War: Forces under U.S. Admiral David Farragut demand the surrender of the Confederate city of New Orleans, Louisiana.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War)

📡 1961 – Robert Noyce is granted a patent for an integrated circuit.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Noyce)

🏙️ 2015 – At least 8,962 are killed in Nepal after a massive 7.8 magnitude earthquake strikes Nepal.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/April_2015_Nepal_earthquake)

⚙️ 1846 – Thornton Affair: Open conflict begins over the disputed border of Texas, triggering the Mexican–American War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thornton_Affair)

💥 1974 – Carnation Revolution: A leftist military coup in Portugal overthrows the authoritarian-conservative Estado Novo regime.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carnation_Revolution)

🕊️ 1981 – More than 100 workers are exposed to radiation during repairs of at the Tsuruga Nuclear Power Plant in Japan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsuruga_Nuclear_Power_Plant)

⚔️ 2005 – A seven-car commuter train derails and crashes into an apartment building near Amagasaki Station in Japan, killing 107, including the driver.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amagasaki_derailment)

🏰 2005 – Bulgaria and Romania sign the Treaty of Accession 2005 to join the European Union.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgaria)
