# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -763 – Assyrians record a solar eclipse that is later used to fix the chronology of Mesopotamian history.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assyria)

🏰 844 – Louis II is crowned as king of Italy at Rome by pope Sergius II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_II_of_Italy)

📜 923 – Battle of Soissons: King Robert I of France is killed and King Charles the Simple is arrested by the supporters of Duke Rudolph of Burgundy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Soissons_(923))

🛡️ 1184 – The naval Battle of Fimreite is won by the Birkebeiner pretender Sverre Sigurdsson. Sigurdsson takes the Norwegian throne and King Magnus V of Norway is killed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Fimreite)

⚓ 1215 – King John of England puts his seal to Magna Carta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John%2C_King_of_England)

🚀 1310 – The Tiepolo conspiracy, seeking to seize power in the Republic of Venice, is thwarted after bloody street clashes in Venice. The suppression of the revolt will lead to the creation of the Council of Ten.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiepolo_conspiracy)

💼 1410 – In a decisive battle at Onon River, the Mongol forces of Oljei Temur were decimated by the Chinese armies of the Yongle Emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yongle_Emperor's_campaigns_against_the_Mongols)

🎖️ 1520 – Pope Leo X threatens to excommunicate Martin Luther in Exsurge Domine.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Leo_X)

📡 1607 – Virginia colonists finish building James's Fort, to defend against Spanish and Native American attacks.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jamestown%2C_Virginia)

🏙️ 1896 – One of the deadliest tsunamis in Japan's history kills more than 22,000 people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1896_Sanriku_earthquake)

⚙️ 1977 – After the death of dictator Francisco Franco in 1975, the first democratic elections take place in Spain.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Death_and_funeral_of_Francisco_Franco)

💥 1804 – New Hampshire approves the Twelfth Amendment to the United States Constitution, ratifying the document.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Hampshire)

🕊️ 1859 – Ambiguity in the Oregon Treaty leads to the "Northwestern Boundary Dispute" between American and British/Canadian settlers.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pig_War_(1859))

⚔️ 1919 – John Alcock and Arthur Brown complete the first nonstop transatlantic flight when they reach Clifden, County Galway, Ireland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Alcock_(RAF_officer))

🏰 1846 – The Oregon Treaty extends the border between the United States and British North America, established by the Treaty of 1818, westward to the Pacific Ocean.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oregon_Treaty)
