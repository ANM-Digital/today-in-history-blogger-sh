# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 193 – The Roman Senate passes a motion proclaiming Septimius Severus emperor, awards divine honours to Pertinax, and sentences current emperor Didius Julianus to death.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Senate)

🏰 1252 – Alfonso X is proclaimed king of Castile and León.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfonso_X_of_Castile)

📜 1412 – The Treaty of Lubowla between the kingdoms of Hungary and Poland is confirmed with one of the largest and magnificent royal meetings and feasts ever held in Buda. King Sigismund of Hungary host king Wladyslaw II Jagiello while several thousand nobles attend.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Lubowla)

🛡️ 1533 – Anne Boleyn is crowned Queen of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anne_Boleyn)

⚓ 1648 – The Roundheads defeat the Cavaliers at the Battle of Maidstone in the Second English Civil War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roundhead)

🚀 1773 – Wolraad Woltemade rescues 14 sailors at the Cape of Good Hope from the sinking ship De Jonge Thomas by riding his horse into the sea seven times. Both he and his horse, Vonk, are drowned on his eighth attempt.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wolraad_Woltemade)

💼 1919 – Prohibition comes into force in Finland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prohibition)

🎖️ 1890 – The United States Census Bureau begins using Herman Hollerith's tabulating machine to count census returns.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau)

📡 1918 – World War I: Western Front: Battle of Belleau Wood: Allied Forces under John J. Pershing and James Harbord engage Imperial German Forces under Wilhelm, German Crown Prince.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I)

🏙️ 1813 – Capture of USS Chesapeake.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capture_of_USS_Chesapeake)

⚙️ 1946 – Ion Antonescu, "Conducator" ("Leader") of Romania during World War II, is executed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ion_Antonescu)

💥 1854 – Åland War: The British navy destroys merchant ships and about 16,000 tar barrels of the wholesale stocks area in Oulu, Grand Duchy of Finland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%85land_War)

🕊️ 1999 – American Airlines Flight 1420 slides and crashes while landing at Little Rock National Airport, killing 11 people on a flight from Dallas to Little Rock.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Airlines_Flight_1420)

⚔️ 1815 – Napoleon promulgates a revised Constitution after it passes a plebiscite.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Napoleon)

🏰 2011 – A rare tornado outbreak occurs in New England; a strong EF3 tornado strikes Springfield, Massachusetts, during the event, killing four people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2011_Springfield_tornado)
