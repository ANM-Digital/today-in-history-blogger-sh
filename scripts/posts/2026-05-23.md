# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 1040 – The Seljuks defeat the Ghaznavids in the battle of Dandanaqan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seljuk_dynasty)

🏰 1308 – Robert the Bruce, king of Scots, defeats John Comyn, earl of Buchan, decisively in the battle of Inverurie.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_the_Bruce)

📜 1430 – Joan of Arc is captured at the Siege of Compiègne by troops from the Burgundian faction.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joan_of_Arc)

🛡️ 1533 – The marriage of King Henry VIII to Catherine of Aragon is declared null and void.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_VIII)

⚓ 1609 – Official ratification of the Second Virginia Charter takes place.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Virginia_Charter)

🚀 1706 – John Churchill, 1st Duke of Marlborough, defeats a French army under Marshal François de Neufville, duc de Villeroy at the Battle of Ramillies.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Churchill%2C_1st_Duke_of_Marlborough)

💼 1863 – The General German Workers' Association, a precursor of the modern Social Democratic Party of Germany, is founded in Leipzig, Kingdom of Saxony.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_German_Workers'_Association)

🎖️ 2016 – Two suicide bombings, conducted by the Islamic State of Iraq and Syria, kill at least 45 potential army recruits in Aden, Yemen.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_State)

📡 1905 – Abdul Hamid II, Sultan of the Ottoman Empire, publicly announces the creation of the Ullah millet for the Aromanians of the empire, which had been established one day earlier. For this reason, the Aromanian National Day is usually celebrated on May 23, although some do so on May 22 instead.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abdul_Hamid_II)

🏙️ 1998 – The Good Friday Agreement is accepted in a referendum in Northern Ireland with roughly 75% voting yes.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Good_Friday_Agreement)

⚙️ 1992 – Italy's most prominent anti-mafia judge Giovanni Falcone, his wife and three body guards are killed by the Corleonesi clan with a half-ton bomb near Capaci, Sicily. His friend and colleague Paolo Borsellino will be assassinated less than two months later, making 1992 a turning point in the history of Italian Mafia prosecutions.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giovanni_Falcone)

💥 2015 – At least 30 people are killed as a result of floods and tornadoes in Texas, Oklahoma, and northern Mexico.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tornado_outbreak_and_floods_of_May_23%E2%80%9326%2C_2015)

🕊️ 2006 – Alaskan stratovolcano Mount Cleveland erupts.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stratovolcano)

⚔️ 1980 – The Shining, the psychological horror film directed by Stanley Kubrick, is premiered on 10 screens in New York City and Los Angeles on the Memorial Day weekend.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Shining_(film))

🏰 1873 – The Canadian Parliament establishes the North-West Mounted Police, the forerunner of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North-West_Mounted_Police)
