# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 1080 – Harald III of Denmark dies and is succeeded by Canute IV, who would later be the first Dane to be canonized.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harald_Hen)

🏰 1349 – The rule of the Bavand dynasty in Mazandaran is brought to an end by the murder of Hasan II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bavand_dynasty)

📜 1492 – Spain and Christopher Columbus sign the Capitulations of Santa Fe for his voyage to Asia to acquire spices.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christopher_Columbus)

🛡️ 1521 – Trial of Martin Luther over his teachings begins during the assembly of the Diet of Worms. Initially intimidated, he asks for time to reflect before answering and is given a stay of one day.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martin_Luther)

⚓ 1783 – American Revolutionary War: Colbert's Raid: A Spanish garrison under Captain Jacobo du Breuil defeat British irregulars at Arkansas Post.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War)

🚀 2006 – A Palestinian suicide bomber detonates an explosive device in a Tel Aviv restaurant, killing 11 people and injuring 70.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palestinians)

💼 1864 – American Civil War: The Battle of Plymouth begins: Confederate forces attack Plymouth, North Carolina.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Plymouth_(1864))

🎖️ 1944 – Forces of the Communist-controlled Greek People's Liberation Army attack the smaller National and Social Liberation resistance group, which surrenders. Its leader Dimitrios Psarros is murdered.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ELAS)

📡 1964 – Jerrie Mock completes the first around-the-world airplane flight by a woman. Her solo flight in the Spirit of Columbus, which took 29 1/2 days, took off and landed at the Port Columbus International Airport in Ohio.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jerrie_Mock)

🏙️ 1907 – The Ellis Island immigration center processes 11,747 people, more than on any other day.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ellis_Island)

⚙️ 1941 – World War II: The Axis powers invasion of Yugoslavia is completed when it signs an armistice with Germany and Italy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

💥 2021 – The funeral of Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, takes place at St George's Chapel, Windsor Castle.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Death_and_funeral_of_Prince_Philip%2C_Duke_of_Edinburgh)

🕊️ 1946 – The last French troops are withdrawn from Syria.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Independence_Day_(Syria))

⚔️ 1982 – Constitution Act, 1982 Patriation of the Canadian constitution in Ottawa by Proclamation of Elizabeth II, Queen of Canada.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitution_Act%2C_1982)

🏰 1961 – Bay of Pigs Invasion: A group of Cuban exiles financed and trained by the CIA lands at the Bay of Pigs in Cuba with the aim of ousting Fidel Castro.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bay_of_Pigs_Invasion)
