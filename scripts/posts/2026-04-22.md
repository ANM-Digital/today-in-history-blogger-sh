# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 960 – Basil II is crowned co-Emperor of the Byzantine Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Basil_II)

🏰 1073 – Election of Pope Gregory VII following the death of Pope Alexander II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Gregory_VII)

📜 1500 – Portuguese navigator Pedro Álvares Cabral lands in Brazil (discovery of Brazil).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pedro_%C3%81lvares_Cabral)

🛡️ 1970 – The first Earth Day is celebrated.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth_Day)

⚓ 1970 – Chicano residents in San Diego, California occupy a site under the Coronado Bridge, leading to the creation of Chicano Park.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Diego)

🚀 1974 – Pan Am Flight 812 crashes on approach to Ngurah Rai International Airport in Denpasar, Bali, Indonesia, killing all 107 people on board.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pan_Am_Flight_812)

💼 2025 – At least 26 people are killed in a terrorist attack on a group of tourists in Pahalgam, Jammu and Kashmir. The Resistance Front (TRF), an offshoot of the Pakistan-based militant group Lashkar-e-Taiba claimed responsibility for the attack.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2025_Pahalgam_attack)

🎖️ 1992 – A series of gas explosions rip through the streets in Guadalajara, Mexico, killing 206.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gas_explosion)

📡 1945 – World War II: Sachsenhausen concentration camp is liberated by soldiers of the Red Army and Polish First Army.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sachsenhausen_concentration_camp)

🏙️ 1944 – World War II: Operation Persecution is initiated: Allied forces land in the Hollandia (currently known as Jayapura) area of New Guinea.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Landing_at_Aitape)

⚙️ 1898 – Spanish–American War: President William McKinley calls for 125,000 volunteers to join the National Guard and fight in Cuba, while Congress more than doubles regular Army forces to 65,000.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish%E2%80%93American_War)

💥 1906 – The 1906 Intercalated Games open in Athens.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1906_Intercalated_Games)

🕊️ 1954 – Red Scare: Witnesses begin testifying and live television coverage of the Army–McCarthy hearings begins.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/McCarthyism)

⚔️ 1944 – World War II: In Greenland, the Allied Sledge Patrol attack the German Bassgeiger weather station.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greenland_in_World_War_II)

🏰 1969 – British yachtsman Sir Robin Knox-Johnston wins the Sunday Times Golden Globe Race and completes the first solo non-stop circumnavigation of the world.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robin_Knox-Johnston)
