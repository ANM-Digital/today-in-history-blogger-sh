# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 51 – Nero, later to become Roman emperor, is given the title princeps iuventutis (head of the youth).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_51)

🏰 306 – Martyrdom of Saint Adrian of Nicomedia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martyr)

📜 581 – Yang Jian declares himself Emperor Wen of Sui, ending the Northern Zhou and beginning the Sui dynasty.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Wen_of_Sui)

🛡️ 852 – Croatian Knez Trpimir I issues a statute, a document with the first known written mention of the Croats name in Croatian sources.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Knyaz)

⚓ 938 – Translation of the relics of martyr Wenceslaus I, Duke of Bohemia, Prince of the Czechs.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Relic)

🚀 1152 – Frederick I Barbarossa is elected King of Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederick_Barbarossa)

💼 1238 – The Battle of the Sit River begins two centuries of Mongol horde domination of Rus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_the_Sit_River)

🎖️ 1351 – Ramathibodi becomes King of Siam.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uthong)

📡 1461 – Wars of the Roses in England: Lancastrian King Henry VI is deposed by his House of York cousin, who then becomes King Edward IV.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wars_of_the_Roses)

🏙️ 1519 – Hernán Cortés arrives in Mexico in search of the Aztec civilization and its wealth.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hern%C3%A1n_Cort%C3%A9s)

⚙️ 1628 – The Massachusetts Bay Colony is granted a Royal charter.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massachusetts_Bay_Colony)

💥 1769 – Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart departs Italy after the last of his three tours there.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wolfgang_Amadeus_Mozart)

🕊️ 2009 – The International Criminal Court (ICC) issues an arrest warrant for Sudanese President Omar Hassan al-Bashir for war crimes and crimes against humanity in Darfur. Al-Bashir is the first sitting head of state to be indicted by the ICC since its establishment in 2002.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Criminal_Court)

⚔️ 1813 – Cyril VI of Constantinople is elected Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyril_VI_of_Constantinople)

🏰 1899 – Cyclone Mahina sweeps in north of Cooktown, Queensland, with a 12 metres (39 ft) wave that reaches up to 5 kilometres (3.1 mi) inland, killing over 300.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyclone_Mahina)
