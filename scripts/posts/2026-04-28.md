# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 224 – The Battle of Hormozdgan is fought. Ardashir I defeats and kills Artabanus V, effectively ending the Parthian Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Hormozdgan)

🏰 357 – Emperor Constantius II enters Rome for the first time to celebrate his victory over Magnus Magnentius.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantius_II)

📜 1192 – Assassination of Conrad of Montferrat (Conrad I), King of Jerusalem, in Tyre, two days after his title to the throne is confirmed by election. The killing is carried out by Hashshashin.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conrad_of_Montferrat)

🛡️ 1253 – Nichiren, a Japanese Buddhist monk, propounds Namu Myōhō Renge Kyō for the first time and declares it to be the essence of Buddhism, in effect founding Nichiren Buddhism.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nichiren)

⚓ 1503 – The Battle of Cerignola is fought. It is noted as one of the first European battles in history won by small arms fire using gunpowder.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Cerignola)

🚀 1611 – Establishment of the Pontifical and Royal University of Santo Tomas, the Catholic University of the Philippines and the largest Catholic university in the world.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Santo_Tomas)

💼 1738 – Pope Clement XII issues the first papal condemnation of Freemasonry and formally prohibits Catholics from becoming Freemasons.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Clement_XII)

🎖️ 1952 – The Treaty of San Francisco comes into effect, restoring Japanese sovereignty and ending its state of war with most of the Allies of World War II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_San_Francisco)

📡 1996 – Port Arthur massacre, Tasmania: A gunman, Martin Bryant, opens fire at the Broad Arrow Cafe in Port Arthur, Tasmania, killing 35 people and wounding 23 others.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Port_Arthur_massacre)

🏙️ 1945 – Benito Mussolini and his mistress Clara Petacci are shot dead by Walter Audisio, a member of the Italian resistance movement.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benito_Mussolini)

⚙️ 1952 – Dwight D. Eisenhower resigns as Supreme Allied Commander of NATO in order to campaign in the 1952 United States presidential election.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dwight_D._Eisenhower)

💥 1881 – Billy the Kid escapes from the Lincoln County jail in Mesilla, New Mexico.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billy_the_Kid)

🕊️ 1949 – The Hukbalahap are accused of assassinating former First Lady of the Philippines Aurora Quezon, while she is en route to dedicate a hospital in memory of her late husband; her daughter and ten others are also killed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hukbalahap)

⚔️ 2004 – CBS News releases evidence of the Abu Ghraib torture and prisoner abuse. The photographs show rape and abuse from the American troops over Iraqi detainees.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CBS_News)

🏰 1859 – The sailing clipper ship Pomona is wrecked on the coast of Ireland with the loss of 424 of the 448 passengers and crew aboard.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomona_(ship))
