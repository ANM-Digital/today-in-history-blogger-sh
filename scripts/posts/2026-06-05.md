# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 830 – Theodora is crowned Byzantine empress and marries then emperor Theophilos in the Hagia Sophia. She is credited with restoring Christian orthodoxy and icons.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodora_(wife_of_Theophilos))

🏰 1086 – Tutush, brother of Seljuk sultan Malik Shah, defeats Suleiman ibn Qutalmish, the Turkish ruler of Anatolia in the battle of Ain Salm.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tutush_I)

📜 1257 – Kraków, in Poland, receives city rights.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Krak%C3%B3w)

🛡️ 1305 – Election of Pope Clement V following the death of Pope Benedict XI in the previous year.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1304%E2%80%931305_conclave)

⚓ 1610 – The masque Tethys' Festival is performed at Whitehall Palace to celebrate the investiture of Henry Frederick, Prince of Wales.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tethys'_Festival)

🚀 1794 – Haitian Revolution: Battle of Port-Républicain: British troops capture the capital of Saint-Domingue.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Haitian_Revolution)

💼 1837 – Houston is incorporated by the Republic of Texas.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Houston)

🎖️ 1968 – Presidential candidate Robert F. Kennedy is assassinated by Sirhan Sirhan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_F._Kennedy)

📡 1989 – The Tank Man halts the progress of a column of advancing tanks for over half an hour after the Tiananmen Square protests of 1989.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tank_Man)

🏙️ 1960 – The Lake Bodom murders occur in Finland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Bodom_murders)

⚙️ 1862 – As the Treaty of Saigon is signed, ceding parts of southern Vietnam to France, the guerrilla leader Trương Định decides to defy Emperor Tự Đức of Vietnam and fight on against the Europeans.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Saigon_(1862))

💥 1976 – The Teton Dam in Idaho, United States, collapses. Eleven people are killed as a result of flooding.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Teton_Dam)

🕊️ 2000 – The Six-Day War in Kisangani begins in Kisangani, in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, between Ugandan and Rwandan forces. A large part of the city is destroyed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Six-Day_War_(2000))

⚔️ 1995 – The Bose–Einstein condensate is first created.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bose%E2%80%93Einstein_condensate)

🏰 1944 – World War II: More than 1,000 British bombers drop 5,000 tons of bombs on German gun batteries on the Normandy coast in preparation for D-Day.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany)
