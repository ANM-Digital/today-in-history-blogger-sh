# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 325 – The First Council of Nicaea is formally opened, starting the first ecumenical council of the Christian Church.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Council_of_Nicaea)

🏰 491 – Empress Ariadne marries Anastasius I. The widowed Augusta is able to choose her successor for the Byzantine throne, after Zeno (late emperor) dies of dysentery.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ariadne_(empress))

📜 685 – The Battle of Dun Nechtain is fought between a Pictish army under King Bridei III and the invading Northumbrians under King Ecgfrith, who are decisively defeated.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Dun_Nechtain)

🛡️ 794 – While visiting the royal Mercian court at Sutton Walls with a view to marrying princess Ælfthryth, King Æthelberht II of East Anglia is taken captive and beheaded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mercia)

⚓ 921 – Christopher Lekapenos is crowned Byzantine co-emperor by his father, emperor Romanos I Lekapenos, on the feast of Whitsun.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christopher_Lekapenos)

🚀 1202 – A major earthquake hits Syria, widely felt between Sicily and Iraq.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1202_Syria_earthquake)

💼 1426 – King Mohnyin Thado formally ascends to the throne of Ava.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mohnyin_Thado)

🎖️ 1520 – Hernan Cortés defeats Pánfilo de Narváez, sent by Spain to punish him for insubordination.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hern%C3%A1n_Cort%C3%A9s)

📡 1609 – Shakespeare's sonnets are first published in London, perhaps illicitly, by the publisher Thomas Thorpe.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shakespeare's_sonnets)

🏙️ 1714 – Johann Sebastian Bach leads the first performance of his cantata for Pentecost, Erschallet, ihr Lieder, BWV 172, at the chapel of Schloss Weimar.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johann_Sebastian_Bach)

⚙️ 1891 – History of cinema: The first public display of Thomas Edison's prototype kinetoscope.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_film)

💥 1949 – In the United States, the Armed Forces Security Agency, the predecessor to the National Security Agency, is established.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Security_Agency)

🕊️ 1969 – The Battle of Hamburger Hill in Vietnam ends.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Hamburger_Hill)

⚔️ 1873 – Levi Strauss and Jacob Davis receive a U.S. patent for blue jeans with copper rivets.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Levi_Strauss)

🏰 1971 – In the Chuknagar massacre, Pakistani forces massacre thousands, mostly Bengali Hindus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chuknagar_massacre)
