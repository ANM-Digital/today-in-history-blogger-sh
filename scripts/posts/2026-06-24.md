# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -1312 – Mursili II launches a campaign against the Kingdom of Azzi-Hayasa.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mur%C5%A1ili_II)

🏰 109 – Roman emperor Trajan inaugurates the Aqua Traiana, an aqueduct that channels water from Lake Bracciano, 40 kilometres (25 miles) northwest of Rome.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_109)

📜 474 – Julius Nepos forces Roman usurper Glycerius to abdicate the throne and proclaims himself Emperor of the Western Roman Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julius_Nepos)

🛡️ 637 – The Battle of Moira is fought between the High King of Ireland and the Kings of Ulster and Dál Riata. It is claimed to be the largest battle in the history of Ireland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Moira)

⚓ 843 – The Vikings sack the French city of Nantes.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vikings_in_Brittany)

🚀 972 – Battle of Cedynia, the first documented victory of Polish forces, takes place.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Cedynia)

💼 1128 – Battle of São Mamede, near Guimarães: Forces led by Afonso I defeat forces led by his mother Teresa of León and her lover Fernando Pérez de Traba.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_S%C3%A3o_Mamede)

🎖️ 1230 – The Siege of Jaén begins, in the context of the Spanish Reconquista.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Ja%C3%A9n_(1230))

📡 1314 – First War of Scottish Independence: The Battle of Bannockburn concludes with a decisive victory by Scottish forces led by Robert the Bruce.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_War_of_Scottish_Independence)

🏙️ 1497 – John Cabot lands in North America at Newfoundland leading the first European exploration of the region since the Vikings.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Cabot)

⚙️ 1509 – Henry VIII and Catherine of Aragon are crowned King and Queen of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_VIII)

💥 1604 – Samuel de Champlain encounters the mouth of the Saint John River, site of Reversing Falls and the present-day city of Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel_de_Champlain)

🕊️ 1713 – The treaty of Adrianople ends the Russo-Ottoman War of 1711–1713, which restores territory to the Ottomans while allowing the Russians to focus on their war against the Swedes.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Adrianople_(1713))

⚔️ 1939 – Siam is renamed Thailand by Plaek Phibunsongkhram, the country's third prime minister.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plaek_Phibunsongkhram)

🏰 1943 – US military police attempt to arrest a black soldier in Bamber Bridge, England, sparking the Battle of Bamber Bridge mutiny that leaves one dead and seven wounded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bamber_Bridge)
