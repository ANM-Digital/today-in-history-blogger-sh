# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -46 – Julius Caesar defeats Caecilius Metellus Scipio and Marcus Porcius Cato (Cato the Younger) at the Battle of Thapsus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julius_Caesar)

🏰 402 – Stilicho defeats the Visigoths under Alaric in the Battle of Pollentia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stilicho)

📜 945 – Byzantine Emperor Constantine VII crowns his son Romanos II as co-emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Byzantine_emperors)

🛡️ 1320 – The Scots reaffirm their independence by signing the Declaration of Arbroath.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Declaration_of_Arbroath)

⚓ 1453 – Mehmed II begins his siege of Constantinople. The city falls on May 29 and is renamed Istanbul.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mehmed_II)

🚀 1580 – One of the largest earthquakes recorded in the history of England, Flanders, or Northern France, takes place.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1580_Dover_Straits_earthquake)

💼 1652 – At the Cape of Good Hope, Dutch sailor Jan van Riebeeck establishes a resupply camp that eventually becomes Cape Town.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cape_of_Good_Hope)

🎖️ 1712 – The New York Slave Revolt of 1712 begins near Broadway.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_Slave_Revolt_of_1712)

📡 1968 – In the downtown district of Richmond, Indiana, a double explosion kills 41 and injures 150.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richmond%2C_Indiana)

🏙️ 1808 – John Jacob Astor incorporates the American Fur Company, that would eventually make him America's first millionaire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Jacob_Astor)

⚙️ 1800 – The Treaty of Constantinople establishes the Septinsular Republic, the first autonomous Greek state since the Fall of the Byzantine Empire. (Under the Old Style calendar then still in use in the Ottoman Empire, the treaty was signed on 21 March.). [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Constantinople_(1800))

💥 1947 – The first Tony Awards are presented for theatrical achievement.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tony_Awards)

🕊️ 2005 – Kurdish leader Jalal Talabani becomes Iraqi president; Shiite Arab Ibrahim al-Jaafari is named premier the next day.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jalal_Talabani)

⚔️ 1948 – The Finno-Soviet Treaty is signed in Moscow.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finno-Soviet_Treaty_of_1948)

🏰 1860 – The Reorganized Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints, later renamed Community of Christ, is organized by Joseph Smith III and others at Amboy, Illinois.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Community_of_Christ)
