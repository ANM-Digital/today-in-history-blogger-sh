# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 913 – Constantine VII, the eight-year-old illegitimate son of Leo VI the Wise, becomes nominal ruler of the Byzantine Empire under the regency of a seven-man council headed by Patriarch Nicholas Mystikos, appointed by Constantine's uncle Alexander on his deathbed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantine_VII)

🏰 1505 – The M8.2–8.8 Lo Mustang earthquake affects Tibet and Nepal, causing severe damage in Kathmandu and parts of the Indo-Gangetic plain.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1505_Lo_Mustang_earthquake)

📜 1654 – Swedish Queen Christina abdicates her throne in favour of her cousin Charles Gustav and converts to Catholicism.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christina%2C_Queen_of_Sweden)

🛡️ 1762 – Seven Years' War: British forces begin the Siege of Havana and temporarily capture the city.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seven_Years'_War)

⚓ 1982 – 1982 Lebanon War: The war begins as forces under Israeli Defense Minister Ariel Sharon invade southern Lebanon during Operation Peace for the Galilee, eventually reaching as far north as the capital Beirut.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1982_Lebanon_War)

🚀 1985 – The grave of "Wolfgang Gerhard" is opened in Embu, Brazil; the exhumed remains are later proven to be those of Josef Mengele, Auschwitz's "Angel of Death"; Mengele is thought to have drowned while swimming in February 1979.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Embu_das_Artes)

💼 1933 – The first drive-in theater opens in Camden, New Jersey.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drive-in_theater)

🎖️ 1944 – World War II: Capture of the Caen canal and Orne river bridges by Allied paratroopers, also known as Operation Coup de Main (incorrectly referred to as Operation Deadstick.). [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capture_of_the_Caen_Canal_and_Orne_River_bridges)

📡 1862 – American Civil War: The First Battle of Memphis, a naval engagement fought on the Mississippi River, results in the capture of Memphis, Tennessee by Union forces from the Confederates.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War)

🏙️ 1822 – Alexis St Martin is accidentally shot in the stomach, leading to William Beaumont's studies on digestion.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexis_St._Martin)

⚙️ 1994 – China Northwest Airlines Flight 2303 crashes near Xi'an Xianyang International Airport, killing all 160 people on board.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China_Northwest_Airlines_Flight_2303)

💥 1993 – Punsalmaagiin Ochirbat wins the first presidential election in Mongolia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Punsalmaagiin_Ochirbat)

🕊️ 1889 – The Great Seattle Fire destroys all of downtown Seattle.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Seattle_Fire)

⚔️ 2023 – Russo-Ukrainian war: The Kakhovka Dam is destroyed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Ukrainian_war)

🏰 1971 – Hughes Airwest Flight 706 collides with a McDonnell Douglas F-4 Phantom II of the United States Marine Corps over the San Gabriel Mountains, killing 50.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hughes_Airwest_Flight_706)
