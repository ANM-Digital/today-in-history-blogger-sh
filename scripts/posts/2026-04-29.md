# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 801 – An earthquake in the Central Apennines hits Rome and Spoleto, damaging the basilica of San Paolo Fuori le Mura.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/801_Apennine_earthquake)

🏰 1091 – Battle of Levounion: The Pechenegs are defeated by Byzantine Emperor Alexios I Komnenos.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Levounion)

📜 1429 – Joan of Arc arrives to relieve the Siege of Orléans.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joan_of_Arc)

🛡️ 1521 – Swedish War of Liberation: Swedish troops defeat a Danish force in the Battle of Västerås.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swedish_War_of_Liberation)

⚓ 1760 – French forces commence the siege of Quebec which is held by the British.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Quebec_(1760))

🚀 1861 – Maryland in the American Civil War: Maryland's House of Delegates votes not to secede from the Union.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maryland_in_the_American_Civil_War)

💼 1991 – A cyclone strikes the Chittagong district of southeastern Bangladesh with winds of around 155 miles per hour (249 km/h), killing at least 138,000 people and leaving as many as ten million homeless.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1991_Bangladesh_cyclone)

🎖️ 1953 – The first U.S. experimental 3D television broadcast shows an episode of Space Patrol on Los Angeles ABC affiliate KECA-TV.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/3D_television)

📡 1985 – Space Shuttle Challenger is launched on STS-51-B.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Challenger)

🏙️ 1862 – American Civil War: The Capture of New Orleans by Union forces under David Farragut.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War)

⚙️ 1970 – Vietnam War: United States and South Vietnamese forces invade Cambodia to interdict the Ho Chi Minh Trail in an attempt to cut off supplies to the Viet Cong and the North Vietnamese Army.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam_War)

💥 1910 – The Parliament of the United Kingdom passes the People's Budget, the first budget in British history with the expressed intent of redistributing wealth among the British public.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliament_of_the_United_Kingdom)

🕊️ 1864 – Theta Xi fraternity is founded at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, the only fraternity to be founded during the American Civil War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theta_Xi)

⚔️ 1992 – Riots in Los Angeles begin, following the acquittal of police officers charged with excessive force in the beating of Rodney King. Over the next three days 63 people are killed and hundreds of buildings are destroyed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1992_Los_Angeles_riots)

🏰 2004 – The final Oldsmobile is built in Lansing, Michigan, ending 107 years of vehicle production.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oldsmobile)
