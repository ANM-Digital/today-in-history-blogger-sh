# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 910 – Battle of Augsburg: The Hungarians defeat the East Frankish army under King Louis the Child, using the famous feigned retreat tactic of the nomadic warriors.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Lechfeld_(910))

🏰 1042 – Constantine IX Monomachos is crowned as Byzantine Emperor, one day after his marriage to Empress Zoe Porphyrogenita.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantine_IX_Monomachos)

📜 1206 – The Ghurid general Qutb ud-Din Aibak founds the Delhi Sultanate.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghurid_dynasty)

🛡️ 1381 – Peasants' Revolt: In England, rebels assemble at Blackheath, just outside London.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peasants'_Revolt)

⚓ 1418 – Armagnac–Burgundian Civil War: Parisians slaughter sympathizers of Bernard VII, Count of Armagnac, along with all prisoners, foreign bankers, and students and faculty of the College of Navarre.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armagnac%E2%80%93Burgundian_Civil_War)

🚀 1550 – The city of Helsinki, Finland (belonging to Sweden at the time) is founded by King Gustav I of Sweden.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Helsinki)

💼 1643 – The Westminster Assembly is convened by the Parliament of England, without the assent of Charles I, in order to restructure the Church of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Westminster_Assembly)

🎖️ 1758 – French and Indian War: Siege of Louisbourg: James Wolfe's attack at Louisbourg, Nova Scotia, commences.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_and_Indian_War)

📡 1944 – World War II: Battle of Carentan: American paratroopers of the 101st Airborne Division secure the town of Carentan, Normandy, France.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Carentan)

🏙️ 1898 – Philippine Declaration of Independence: General Emilio Aguinaldo declares the Philippines' independence from Spain.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_Declaration_of_Independence)

⚙️ 1982 – A nuclear disarmament rally and concert is held in New York City.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City)

💥 2025 – Air India Flight 171, a Boeing 787-8 Dreamliner, crashes shortly after takeoff into the B. J. Medical College, Ahmedabad, India, killing 241 out of 242 onboard as well as 19 on the ground. This marked the first fatal crash and hull loss of the Boeing 787 Dreamliner.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_India_Flight_171)

🕊️ 1950 – An Air France Douglas DC-4 crashes near Bahrain International Airport, killing 46 people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1950_Bahrain_Air_France_Douglas_DC-4_crashes)

⚔️ 1940 – World War II: Thirteen thousand British and French troops surrender to General Erwin Rommel at Saint-Valery-en-Caux.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

🏰 1975 – State of Uttar Pradesh v. Raj Narain: Judge Jagmohanlal Sinha rules against Indira Gandhi in a case on her election to the Indian Parliament, and that she should be banned from holding any public office, triggering a political crisis.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_of_Uttar_Pradesh_v._Raj_Narain)
