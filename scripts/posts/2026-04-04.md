# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -503 – Roman consul Agrippa Menenius Lanatus celebrates a triumph for a military victory over the Sabines.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_consul)

🏰 190 – Dong Zhuo has his troops evacuate the capital Luoyang and burn it to the ground.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dong_Zhuo)

📜 611 – Maya king Uneh Chan of Calakmul sacks rival city-state Palenque in southern Mexico.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scroll_Serpent)

🛡️ 801 – King Louis the Pious captures Barcelona from the Moors after a siege of several months.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_the_Pious)

⚓ 1268 – A five-year Byzantine–Venetian peace treaty is concluded between Venetian envoys and Emperor Michael VIII Palaiologos.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine%E2%80%93Venetian_treaty_of_1268)

🚀 1423 – Death of the Venetian Doge Tommaso Mocenigo, under whose rule victories were achieved against the Kingdom of Hungary and against the Ottoman Empire at the Battle of Gallipoli (1416).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doge_(title))

💼 1581 – Francis Drake is knighted by Queen Elizabeth I for completing a circumnavigation of the world.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_Drake)

🎖️ 1609 – Moriscos are expelled from the Kingdom of Valencia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morisco)

📡 1796 – Georges Cuvier delivers the first paleontological lecture.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georges_Cuvier)

🏙️ 1991 – Senator John Heinz of Pennsylvania and six others are killed when a helicopter collides with their airplane over an elementary school in Merion, Pennsylvania.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Heinz)

⚙️ 1945 – World War II: United States Army troops liberate Ohrdruf forced labor camp in Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Army)

💥 1968 – Apollo program: NASA launches Apollo 6.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_program)

🕊️ 1996 – Comet Hyakutake is imaged by the USA Asteroid Orbiter Near Earth Asteroid Rendezvous.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comet_Hyakutake)

⚔️ 1920 – The four-day Nebi Musa riots commence.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1920_Nebi_Musa_riots)
