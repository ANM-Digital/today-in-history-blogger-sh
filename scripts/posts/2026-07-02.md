# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 311 – Beginning of the papacy of Pope Miltiades.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_311)

🏰 626 – Li Shimin, the future Emperor Taizong of Tang, ambushes and kills his rival brothers Li Yuanji and Li Jiancheng in the Xuanwu Gate Incident.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Taizong_of_Tang)

📜 866 – Battle of Brissarthe: The Franks led by Robert the Strong are defeated by a joint Breton-Viking army.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Brissarthe)

🛡️ 1298 – Battle of Göllheim: Albert I of Habsburg defeats Adolf of Nassau-Weilburg.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_G%C3%B6llheim)

⚓ 1494 – Age of Discovery: The Treaty of Tordesillas is ratified by Spain.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Age_of_Discovery)

🚀 1582 – Battle of Yamazaki: Toyotomi Hideyoshi defeats Akechi Mitsuhide.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Yamazaki)

💼 1644 – English Civil War: Battle of Marston Moor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/English_Civil_War)

🎖️ 1776 – American Revolution: The Continental Congress adopts the Lee Resolution severing ties with the Kingdom of Great Britain. The wording of the formal Declaration of Independence was agreed upon and publicly released two days later, on July 4.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolution)

📡 1997 – The Bank of Thailand floats the baht, triggering the Asian financial crisis.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thai_baht)

🏙️ 1986 – Rodrigo Rojas and Carmen Gloria Quintana are burnt alive during a street demonstration against the dictatorship of General Augusto Pinochet in Chile in the Quemados case.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rodrigo_Rojas_de_Negri)

⚙️ 1921 – World War I: U.S. President Warren G. Harding signs the Knox–Porter Resolution formally ending the war between the United States and Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I)

💥 1863 – American Civil War: On the second day of the Battle of Gettysburg - the Battle of Little Round Top takes place and results in a Union victory after the Confederate troops unsuccessfully try to assault the Union left flank.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War)

🕊️ 2013 – A magnitude 6.1 earthquake strikes Aceh, Indonesia, killing at least 42 people and injuring 420 others.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2013_Aceh_earthquake)

⚔️ 1964 – Civil rights movement: U.S. President Lyndon B. Johnson signs the Civil Rights Act of 1964 meant to prohibit segregation in public places.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Civil_rights_movement)

🏰 1937 – Amelia Earhart and navigator Fred Noonan are last heard from over the Pacific Ocean and disappear while attempting to make the first equatorial round-the-world flight.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amelia_Earhart)
