# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -474 – Roman consul Aulus Manlius Vulso celebrates an ovation for concluding the war against Veii and securing a forty years' truce.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_consul)

🏰 -200 – The Roman Republic under its new consuls Publius Sulpicius Galba and Gaius Aurelius Cotta declares war on Philip V of Macedon, starting the Second Macedonian War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Republic)

📜 -44 – The assassination of Julius Caesar, the dictator of the Roman Republic, by a group of senators takes place on the Ides of March.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assassination_of_Julius_Caesar)

🛡️ 351 – Constantius Gallus is elevated as Caesar and then sent to Antioch to govern the Roman East.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantius_Gallus)

⚓ 493 – Odoacer, the first barbarian King of Italy after the fall of the Western Roman Empire, is slain by Theoderic the Great, king of the Ostrogoths, while the two kings were feasting together.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Odoacer)

🚀 856 – Michael III, emperor of the Byzantine Empire, overthrows the regency of his mother, empress Theodora (wife of Theophilos) with support of the Byzantine nobility.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_III)

💼 933 – After a ten-year truce, German King Henry the Fowler defeats a Hungarian army at the Battle of Riade near the Unstrut river.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_the_Fowler)

🎖️ 1311 – Battle of Halmyros: The Catalan Company defeats Walter V, Count of Brienne to take control of the Duchy of Athens, a Crusader state in Greece.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Halmyros)

📡 1412 – Treaty of Lublowa: After the Peace of Thorn, Grand Master Heinrich von Plauen asks Sigismund of Hungary for economic aid. Sigismund agrees to mediate reduction to the third installment, demarcation of the Samogitian border, and other matters with a grand tournament. Hunts and lavish feasts were also organized. Sigismund invited, among others, polish king Wladyslaw Jagiello, Heinrich von Plauen and bosnian king Tvrtko II. There were people from 17 countries and languages - 40.000 nobles and 2000 knights were present from all over Europe, even England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Lubowla)

🏙️ 1564 – Mughal Emperor Akbar abolishes the jizya tax on non-Muslim subjects.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akbar)

⚙️ 1626 – A dam failure causes the sudden flooding of the mining city of Potosí in present-day Bolivia leading to the death of thousands and the massive release of toxic mercury into the environment.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dam_failure)

💥 1783 – In an emotional speech in Newburgh, New York, George Washington asks his officers not to support the Newburgh Conspiracy. The plea is successful, and the threatened coup d'état never takes place.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newburgh%2C_New_York)

🕊️ 1907 – The first parliamentary elections of Finland (at the time the Grand Duchy of Finland) are held.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1907_Finnish_parliamentary_election)

⚔️ 1823 – Sailor Benjamin Morrell erroneously reports the existence of the island of New South Greenland near Antarctica.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin_Morrell)

🏰 2008 – Stockpiles of obsolete ammunition explode at an ex-military ammunition depot in the village of Gërdec, Albania, killing 26 people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2008_G%C3%ABrdec_explosions)
