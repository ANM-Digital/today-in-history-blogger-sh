# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 1158 – The city of Munich is founded by Henry the Lion on the banks of the river Isar.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Munich)

🏰 1216 – First Barons' War: Prince Louis of France takes the city of Winchester, abandoned by John, King of England, and soon conquers over half of the kingdom.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Barons'_War)

📜 1381 – Richard II of England meets leaders of the Peasants' Revolt at Mile End. The Tower of London is stormed by rebels who enter without resistance.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_II_of_England)

🛡️ 1404 – Welsh rebel leader Owain Glyndŵr, having declared himself Prince of Wales, allies himself with the French against King Henry IV of England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Owain_Glynd%C5%B5r)

⚓ 1618 – Joris Veseler prints the first Dutch newspaper Courante uyt Italien, Duytslandt, &c. in Amsterdam (approximate date).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Courante_uyt_Italien%2C_Duytslandt%2C_%26c.)

🚀 1775 – American Revolutionary War: the Continental Army is established by the Continental Congress, marking the birth of the United States Armed Forces.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War)

💼 1951 – UNIVAC I is dedicated by the U.S. Census Bureau.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UNIVAC_I)

🎖️ 1986 – The Mindbender derails, killing three riders and severely injuring one at the Fantasyland (known today as Galaxyland) indoor amusement park at West Edmonton Mall in Edmonton, Alberta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mindbender_(Galaxyland))

📡 1937 – U.S. House of Representatives passes the Marihuana Tax Act.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marihuana_Tax_Act_of_1937)

🏙️ 2014 – A Ukraine military Ilyushin Il-76 airlifter is shot down, killing all 49 people on board.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2014_Ukrainian_Air_Force_Ilyushin_Il-76_shootdown)

⚙️ 1830 – Beginning of the French colonization of Algeria: Thirty-four thousand French soldiers begin their invasion of Algiers, landing 27 kilometers west at Sidi Fredj.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Algeria)

💥 1863 – Second Assault on the Confederate works at the Siege of Port Hudson during the American Civil War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Port_Hudson)

🕊️ 1954 – U.S. President Dwight D. Eisenhower signs a bill into law that places the words "under God" into the United States Pledge of Allegiance.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dwight_D._Eisenhower)

⚔️ 1839 – Henley Royal Regatta: the village of Henley-on-Thames, on the River Thames in Oxfordshire, stages its first regatta.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henley_Royal_Regatta)

🏰 1821 – Badi VII, king of Sennar, surrenders his throne and realm to Ismail Pasha, general of the Ottoman Empire, bringing the 300 year old Sudanese kingdom to an end.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Badi_VII)
