# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -567 – Servius Tullius, the king of Rome, celebrates a triumph for his victory over the Etruscans.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Servius_Tullius)

🏰 -240 – First recorded perihelion passage of Halley's Comet.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apsis)

📜 1085 – Alfonso VI of Castile takes Toledo, Spain, back from the Moors.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfonso_VI_of_Le%C3%B3n_and_Castile)

🛡️ 1420 – Henry the Navigator is appointed governor of the Order of Christ.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_Henry_the_Navigator)

⚓ 1521 – The Diet of Worms ends when Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor, issues the Edict of Worms, declaring Martin Luther an outlaw.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diet_of_Worms)

🚀 1644 – Ming general Wu Sangui forms an alliance with the invading Manchus and opens the gates of the Great Wall of China at Shanhaiguan pass, letting the Manchus through towards the capital Beijing.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ming_dynasty)

💼 1738 – A treaty between Pennsylvania and Maryland ends the Conojocular War with settlement of a boundary dispute and exchange of prisoners.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pennsylvania)

🎖️ 1961 – Apollo program: U.S. President John F. Kennedy announces, before a special joint session of the U.S. Congress, that the United States "should commit itself to achieving the goal, before this decade is out, of landing a man on the Moon and returning him safely to the Earth.". [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_program)

📡 1946 – The Emirate of Transjordan becomes independent from the United Kingdom as the Hashemite Kingdom of Transjordan after ratifying the Treaty of London and making their Amir, Abdullah I, their King.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emirate_of_Transjordan)

🏙️ 1982 – Falklands War: HMS Coventry is sunk by Argentine Air Force A-4 Skyhawks.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Falklands_War)

⚙️ 1878 – Gilbert and Sullivan's comic opera H.M.S. Pinafore opens at the Opera Comique in London.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gilbert_and_Sullivan)

💥 1977 – The Chinese government removes a decade-old ban on William Shakespeare's work, effectively ending the Cultural Revolution started in 1966.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Shakespeare)

🕊️ 1914 – The House of Commons of the United Kingdom passes the Home Rule Bill for devolution in Ireland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/House_of_Commons_of_the_United_Kingdom)

⚔️ 2012 – The SpaceX Dragon 1 becomes the first commercial spacecraft to successfully rendezvous and berth with the International Space Station.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SpaceX_Dragon_1)

🏰 2018 – Ireland votes to repeal the Eighth Amendment of their constitution that prohibits abortion in all but a few cases, choosing to replace it with the Thirty-sixth Amendment of the Constitution of Ireland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Ireland)
