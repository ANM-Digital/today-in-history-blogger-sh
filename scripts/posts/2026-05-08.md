# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -453 – Spring and Autumn period: The house of Zhao defeats the house of Zhi, ending the Battle of Jinyang, a military conflict between the elite families of the State of Jin.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spring_and_Autumn_period)

🏰 413 – Emperor Honorius signs an edict providing tax relief for the Italian provinces Tuscia, Campania, Picenum, Samnium, Apulia, Lucania and Calabria, which were plundered by the Visigoths.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Honorius)

📜 589 – Reccared I opens the Third Council of Toledo, marking the entry of Visigothic Spain into the Catholic Church.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reccared_I)

🛡️ 1360 – Treaty of Brétigny drafted between King Edward III of England and King John II of France (the Good).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Br%C3%A9tigny)

⚓ 1429 – Joan of Arc lifts the Siege of Orléans, turning the tide of the Hundred Years' War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joan_of_Arc)

🚀 1516 – A group of imperial guards, led by Trịnh Duy Sản, murdered Emperor Lê Tương Dực and fled, leaving the capital Thăng Long undefended.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tr%E1%BA%A7n_Cao_rebellion)

💼 1608 – A newly nationalized silver mine in Scotland at Hilderston, West Lothian is re-opened by Bevis Bulmer.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nationalization)

🎖️ 1721 – In the Papal States, Cardinal Michelangelo dei Conti is elected Pope, and takes the name Innocent XIII.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Papal_States)

📡 1945 – The Halifax riot starts when thousands of civilians and servicemen rampage through Halifax, Nova Scotia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Halifax_riot)

🏙️ 1957 – South Vietnamese President Ngo Dinh Diem began a state visit to the United States, his regime's main sponsor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ngo_Dinh_Diem)

⚙️ 1924 – The Klaipėda Convention is signed formally incorporating Klaipėda Region (Memel Territory) into Lithuania.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Klaip%C4%97da_Convention)

💥 1963 – South Vietnamese soldiers under the Roman Catholic President Ngo Dinh Diem open fire on Buddhists defying a ban on the flying of the Buddhist flag on Vesak, killing nine and sparking the Buddhist crisis.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hu%E1%BA%BF_Ph%E1%BA%ADt_%C4%90%E1%BA%A3n_shootings)

🕊️ 1970 – The Beatles release their 12th and final studio album Let It Be.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Beatles)

⚔️ 1950 – The Tollund Man was discovered in a peat bog near Silkeborg, Denmark.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tollund_Man)

🏰 1946 – Estonian schoolgirls Aili Jõgi and Ageeda Paavel blow up the Soviet memorial which preceded the Bronze Soldier of Tallinn.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aili_J%C3%B5gi)
