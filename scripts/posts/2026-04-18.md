# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 796 – King Æthelred I of Northumbria is murdered in Corbridge by a group led by his ealdormen, Ealdred and Wada. The patrician Osbald is crowned, but abdicates within 27 days.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%86thelred_I_of_Northumbria)

🏰 1428 – Peace of Ferrara between Republic of Venice, Duchy of Milan, Republic of Florence and House of Gonzaga: ending of the second campaign of the Wars in Lombardy fought until the Treaty of Lodi in 1454, which will then guarantee the conditions for the development of the Italian Renaissance.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Venice)

📜 1506 – The cornerstone of the current St. Peter's Basilica is laid.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cornerstone)

🛡️ 1689 – Bostonians rise up in rebellion against Sir Edmund Andros.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boston)

⚓ 1738 – Real Academia de la Historia ("Royal Academy of History") is founded in Madrid.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Academy_of_History)

🚀 1909 – Joan of Arc is beatified in Rome.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joan_of_Arc)

💼 1988 – The United States launches Operation Praying Mantis against Iranian naval forces in the largest naval battle since World War II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Praying_Mantis)

🎖️ 2018 – King Mswati III of Swaziland announces that his country's name will change to Eswatini.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_of_Eswatini)

📡 1947 – The Operation Big Bang, the largest non-nuclear man-made explosion to that time, destroys bunkers and military installations on the North Sea island of Heligoland, Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Big_Bang)

🏙️ 1949 – The Republic of Ireland Act comes into force, declaring Éire to be a republic and severing Ireland's "association" with the Commonwealth of Nations.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Republic_of_Ireland_Act_1948)

⚙️ 1996 – The Israeli military commits the Qana massacre in a deliberate shelling of a United Nations compound near the village of Qana in southern Lebanon, killing 106 Lebanese civilians who were taking shelter there and wounding over 100 more.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qana_massacre)

💥 1972 – East African Airways Flight 720 crashes during a rejected takeoff from Addis Ababa Bole International Airport in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, killing 43.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_African_Airways_Flight_720)

🕊️ 1897 – The Greco-Turkish War is declared between Greece and the Ottoman Empire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greco-Turkish_War_(1897))

⚔️ 1945 – World War II: Over 1,000 bombers attack the small island of Heligoland, Germany.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bomber)

🏰 1980 – The Republic of Zimbabwe (formerly Rhodesia) comes into being, with Canaan Banana as the country's first President. The Zimbabwean dollar replaces the Rhodesian dollar as the official currency.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zimbabwe)
