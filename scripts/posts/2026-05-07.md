# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 351 – The Jewish revolt against Constantius Gallus breaks out after his arrival at Antioch.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jewish_revolt_against_Constantius_Gallus)

🏰 558 – In Constantinople, the dome of the Hagia Sophia collapses, twenty years after its construction. Justinian I immediately orders that the dome be rebuilt.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantinople)

📜 1104 – The Seljuk emirs of Mosul and Mardin defeat the Crusader States of Antioch and Edessa.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seljuk_Empire)

🛡️ 1274 – In France, the Second Council of Lyon opens; it ratified a decree to regulate the election of the Pope.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Council_of_Lyon)

⚓ 1342 – In Avignon, France, Cardinal Pierre Roger is elected Pope and takes the name Clement VI.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avignon)

🚀 1487 – The Siege of Málaga commences during the Spanish Reconquista.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_M%C3%A1laga_(1487))

💼 1544 – The Burning of Edinburgh by an English army is the first action of the Rough Wooing.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burning_of_Edinburgh)

🎖️ 1625 – State funeral of James VI and I (1566–1625) is held at Westminster Abbey.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Death_and_funeral_of_James_VI_and_I)

📡 1718 – The city of New Orleans is founded by Jean-Baptiste Le Moyne, Sieur de Bienville.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Orleans)

🏙️ 1952 – The concept of the integrated circuit, the basis for all modern computers, is first published by Geoffrey Dummer.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integrated_circuit)

⚙️ 1895 – In Saint Petersburg, Russian scientist Alexander Stepanovich Popov demonstrates to the Russian Physical and Chemical Society his invention, the Popov lightning detector—a primitive radio receiver. In some parts of the former Soviet Union the anniversary of this day is celebrated as Radio Day.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Petersburg)

💥 1840 – The Great Natchez Tornado strikes Natchez, Mississippi killing 317 people. It is the second deadliest tornado in United States history.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1840_Natchez_tornado)

🕊️ 1864 – American Civil War: The Army of the Potomac, under General Ulysses S. Grant, breaks off from the Battle of the Wilderness and moves southwards.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War)

⚔️ 1937 – Spanish Civil War: The German Condor Legion, equipped with Heinkel He 51 biplanes, arrives in Spain to assist Francisco Franco's forces.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_Civil_War)

🏰 1998 – Mercedes-Benz buys Chrysler for US$40 billion and forms DaimlerChrysler in the largest industrial merger in history.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mercedes-Benz)
