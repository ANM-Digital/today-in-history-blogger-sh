# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -1457 – Battle of Megiddo – the first battle to have been recorded in what is accepted as relatively reliable detail.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1450s_BC)

🏰 69 – Defeated by Vitellius' troops at Bedriacum, Roman emperor Otho commits suicide.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_69)

📜 556 – Pope Pelagius I is consecrated following Imperial approval by Emperor Justinian I.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Papacy)

🛡️ 682 – Pope Leo II is elected head of the Catholic Church, although he will not be consecrated until 17 August.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Leo_II)

⚓ 1346 – Stefan Dušan, "the Mighty", is crowned Emperor of the Serbs at Skopje, his empire occupying much of the Balkans.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stefan_Du%C5%A1an)

🚀 1520 – The Revolt of the Comuneros begins in Spain against the rule of Charles V.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Revolt_of_the_Comuneros)

💼 1746 – The Battle of Culloden is fought between the French-supported Jacobites and the British Hanoverian forces commanded by William Augustus, Duke of Cumberland, in Scotland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Culloden)

🎖️ 1917 – Russian Revolution: Vladimir Lenin returns to Petrograd, Russia, from exile in Switzerland.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Revolution)

📡 1961 – In a nationally broadcast speech, Cuban leader Fidel Castro declares that he is a Marxist–Leninist and that Cuba is going to adopt Communism.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fidel_Castro)

🏙️ 1941 – World War II: The Italian-German Tarigo convoy is attacked and destroyed by British ships.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

⚙️ 1910 – The oldest existing indoor ice hockey arena still used for the sport in the 21st century, Boston Arena, opens for the first time.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ice_hockey)

💥 1853 – The Great Indian Peninsula Railway opens the first passenger rail in India, from Bori Bunder to Thane.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Indian_Peninsula_Railway)

🕊️ 2024 – The historic Børsen in Copenhagen, Denmark, is severely damaged by a fire.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/B%C3%B8rsen)

⚔️ 2013 – A 7.8-magnitude earthquake strikes Sistan and Balochistan province, Iran, killing at least 35 people and injuring 117 others.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moment_magnitude_scale)

🏰 1948 – The Organization of European Economic Co-operation is formed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OECD)
