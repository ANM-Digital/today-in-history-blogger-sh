# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 113 – Roman emperor Trajan dedicates a column celebrating and depicting his victory over the Dacians.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AD_113)

🏰 254 – Pope Stephen I succeeds Pope Lucius I, becoming the 23rd pope of the Catholic Church, and immediately takes a stand against Novatianism.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Stephen_I)

📜 907 – Zhu Wen forces Emperor Ai into abdicating, ending the Tang dynasty after nearly three hundred years of rule.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhu_Wen)

🛡️ 1157 – A church council presided over by Byzantine emperor Manuel I Komnenos in the Palace of Blachernae investigates the orthodoxy of the patriarch of Antioch, Soterichos Panteugenos.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Blachernae_(1157))

⚓ 1328 – Antipope Nicholas V, a claimant to the papacy, is consecrated in Rome by the Bishop of Venice.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antipope_Nicholas_V)

🚀 1497 – Pope Alexander VI excommunicates Girolamo Savonarola.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Alexander_VI)

💼 1510 – The Prince of Anhua rebellion begins when Zhu Zhifan kills all the officials invited to a banquet and declares his intent on ousting the powerful Ming dynasty eunuch Liu Jin during the reign of the Zhengde Emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_of_Anhua_rebellion)

🎖️ 1743 – Maria Theresa of Austria is crowned Queen of Bohemia after defeating her rival, Charles VII, Holy Roman Emperor.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maria_Theresa)

📡 1926 – The Italian-built airship Norge becomes the first vessel to fly over the North Pole.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Norge_(airship))

🏙️ 1821 – The first major battle of the Greek War of Independence against the Turks is fought in Valtetsi.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greek_War_of_Independence)

⚙️ 1862 – American Civil War: Union Army troops occupy Baton Rouge, Louisiana.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War)

💥 1982 – During a procession outside the shrine of the Virgin Mary in Fátima, Portugal, security guards overpower Juan María Fernández y Krohn before he can attack Pope John Paul II with a bayonet.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary%2C_mother_of_Jesus)

🕊️ 1978 – In Zaire, rebels occupy the city of Kolwezi, the mining center of the province of Shaba (now known as Katanga); the local government asks the US, France and Belgium to restore order.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zaire)

⚔️ 2002 – Former US President Jimmy Carter arrives in Cuba for a five-day visit with Fidel Castro, becoming the first President of the United States, in or out of office, to visit the island since the Cuban Revolution.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jimmy_Carter)

🏰 2006 – Iranian Azeris interpret a cartoon published in an Iranian magazine as insulting, resulting in massive riots throughout the country.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iranian_Azerbaijanis)
