# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 1256 – The Augustinian monastic order is constituted at the Lecceto Monastery when Pope Alexander IV issues a papal bull Licet ecclesiae catholicae.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Augustinians)

🏰 1415 – Religious reformer John Wycliffe is condemned as a heretic at the Council of Constance.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Wycliffe)

📜 1626 – Dutch explorer Peter Minuit arrives in New Netherland (present day Manhattan Island) aboard the See Meeuw.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Minuit)

🛡️ 1738 – The Imperial Theatrical School, the first ballet school in Russia, is founded.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vaganova_Academy_of_Russian_Ballet)

⚓ 1953 – Ernest Hemingway wins the Pulitzer Prize for The Old Man and the Sea.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernest_Hemingway)

🚀 1973 – The 108-story Sears Tower in Chicago is topped out at 1,451 feet (442 m) as the world's tallest building.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Willis_Tower)

💼 1859 – The Cornwall Railway opens across the Royal Albert Bridge linking Devon and Cornwall in England.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cornwall_Railway)

🎖️ 1910 – The Royal Canadian Navy is created.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Canadian_Navy)

📡 1932 – Having been incarcerated at the Cook County Jail since his sentencing on October 24, 1931, mobster Al Capone is transferred to the federal penitentiary in Atlanta after the U.S. Supreme Court denies his appeal for conviction of tax evasion.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cook_County_Jail)

🏙️ 2023 – Nine people are killed and thirteen injured in a spree shooting in Mladenovac and Smederevo, Serbia. It is the second mass shooting in the country in two days.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mladenovac_and_Smederevo_shootings)

⚙️ 1989 – Iran–Contra affair: Former White House aide Oliver North is convicted of three crimes and acquitted of nine other charges; the convictions are later overturned on appeal.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran%E2%80%93Contra_affair)

💥 1946 – In San Francisco Bay, U.S. Marines from the nearby Treasure Island Naval Base stop a two-day riot at Alcatraz Federal Penitentiary.  Five people are killed in the riot.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Francisco_Bay)

🕊️ 1961 – Malcolm Ross and Victor Prather attain a new altitude record for manned balloon flight ascending in the Strato-Lab V open gondola to 113,740 feet (34.67 km).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malcolm_Ross_(balloonist))

⚔️ 1823 – Brazilian War of Independence: A Brazilian squadron led by Lord Cochrane engages a Portuguese squadron under João de Campos off Salvador, Bahia.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazilian_War_of_Independence)

🏰 1982 – Twenty sailors are killed when the British Type 42 destroyer HMS Sheffield is hit by an Argentinian Exocet missile during the Falklands War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Type_42_destroyer)
