# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -217 – A Carthaginian force under Hannibal ambushes and defeats a Roman army commanded by Gaius Flaminius in the battle of lake Trasimene during the Second Punic War.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ancient_Carthage)

🏰 533 – A Byzantine expeditionary fleet under Belisarios sails from Constantinople to attack the Vandals in Africa, via Greece and Sicily.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belisarius)

📜 870 – Caliph al-Muhtadi is killed by rebellious troops and is succeeded by his cousin Al-Mu'tamid.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Muhtadi)

🛡️ 1307 – Külüg Khan is enthroned as Khagan of the Mongols and Wuzong of the Yuan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K%C3%BCl%C3%BCg_Khan)

⚓ 1529 – French forces are driven out of northern Italy by Spain at the Battle of Landriano during the War of the League of Cognac.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_France)

🚀 1621 – Execution of 27 Czech noblemen on the Old Town Square in Prague as a consequence of the Battle of White Mountain.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_Town_Square_execution)

💼 1734 – In Montreal, New France, a  slave known by the French name of Marie-Joseph Angélique is put to death, having been convicted of setting the fire that destroyed much of the city.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Montreal)

🎖️ 2012 – An Indonesian Air Force Fokker F27 Friendship crashes near Halim Perdanakusuma International Airport, killing 11.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesian_Air_Force)

📡 1973 – In its decision in Miller v. California, 413 U.S. 15, the Supreme Court of the United States establishes the Miller test for determining whether something is obscene and not protected speech under the U.S. constitution.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miller_v._California)

🏙️ 1970 – Penn Central declares Section 77 bankruptcy in what was the largest U.S. corporate bankruptcy to date.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Penn_Central_Transportation_Company)

⚙️ 1989 – The U.S. Supreme Court rules in Texas v. Johnson, 491 U.S. 397, that American flag-burning is a form of political protest protected by the First Amendment.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States)

💥 1964 – Three civil rights workers, Andrew Goodman, James Chaney and Michael Schwerner, are murdered in Neshoba County, Mississippi, United States, by members of the Ku Klux Klan.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Civil_rights_movement)

🕊️ 1941 – Having defeated forces of Vichy France, Allied forces capture Damascus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vichy_France)

⚔️ 2025 – A hot air balloon catches fire mid-flight and crashes in Praia Grande, Santa Catarina, Brazil, killing 8 of the 21 on board.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_air_balloon)

🏰 1813 – Peninsular War: Wellington defeats Joseph Bonaparte at the Battle of Vitoria.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peninsular_War)
