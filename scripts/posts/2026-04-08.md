# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 217 – Roman emperor Caracalla is assassinated and is succeeded by his Praetorian Guard prefect, Marcus Opellius Macrinus.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_emperor)

🏰 876 – The Battle of Dayr al-'Aqul saves Baghdad from the Saffarids.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Dayr_al-Aqul)

📜 1139 – Roger II of Sicily is excommunicated by Innocent II for supporting Anacletus II as pope for seven years, even though Roger had already publicly recognized Innocent's claim to the papacy.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roger_II_of_Sicily)

🛡️ 1232 – Mongol–Jin War: The Mongols begin their siege on Kaifeng, the capital of the Jin dynasty.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mongol_conquest_of_the_Jin_dynasty)

⚓ 1605 – The city of Oulu, Finland, is founded by Charles IX of Sweden.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oulu)

🚀 1730 – Shearith Israel, the first synagogue in continental North America, is dedicated.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Congregation_Shearith_Israel)

💼 2024 – Solar eclipse: A total solar eclipse takes place at the Moon's ascending node, visible across North America.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_April_8%2C_2024)

🎖️ 2010 – U.S. President Barack Obama and Russian President Dmitry Medvedev sign the New START Treaty.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barack_Obama)

📡 1959 – A team of computer manufacturers, users, and university people led by Grace Hopper meets to discuss the creation of a new programming language that would be called COBOL.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grace_Hopper)

🏙️ 1954 – South African Airways Flight 201: A de Havilland DH.106 Comet 1 crashes into the sea during night killing 21 people.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_African_Airways_Flight_201)

⚙️ 2005 – A solar eclipse occurs, visible over areas of the Pacific Ocean and Latin American countries such as Costa Rica, Panama, Colombia and Venezuela.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_April_8%2C_2005)

💥 2002 – The Space Shuttle Atlantis is launched on mission STS-110, carrying the S0 truss to the International Space Station. Astronaut Jerry L. Ross also becomes the first person to fly on seven spaceflights.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Atlantis)

🕊️ 1968 – BOAC Flight 712 catches fire shortly after takeoff. As a result of her actions in the accident, Barbara Jane Harrison is awarded a posthumous George Cross, the only GC awarded to a woman in peacetime.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BOAC_Flight_712)

⚔️ 1974 – Hank Aaron passes Babe Ruth as the all-time leader in career home runs by hitting his 715th home run off of Al Downing at Atlanta-Fulton County Stadium.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hank_Aaron)

🏰 2020 – Bernie Sanders ends his presidential campaign, leaving Joe Biden as the Democratic Party's nominee.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bernie_Sanders)
