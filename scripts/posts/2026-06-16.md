# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 632 – Yazdegerd III ascends the throne as king (shah) of the Persian Empire. He becomes the last ruler of the Sasanian dynasty (modern Iran).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yazdegerd_III)

🏰 1407 – Ming–Hồ War: Retired King Hồ Quý Ly and his son King Hồ Hán Thương of Hồ dynasty are captured by the Ming armies.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ming_conquest_of_%C4%90%E1%BA%A1i_Ngu)

📜 1632 – The Plymouth Company grants a land patent to Thomas Purchase, the first settler of Pejepscot, Maine, settling at the site of Fort Andross.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plymouth_Company)

🛡️ 1745 – War of the Austrian Succession: New England colonial troops under the command of William Pepperrell capture the Fortress of Louisbourg in Louisbourg, New France (Old Style date).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_the_Austrian_Succession)

⚓ 1925 – Artek, the most famous Young Pioneer camp of the Soviet Union, is established.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artek_(camp))

🚀 1824 – A meeting at Old Slaughter's coffee house in London leads to the formation of what is now the Royal Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (RSPCA).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RSPCA)

💼 1940 – The Soviet Union occupies Lithuania, which will eventually become the Lithuanian Soviet Socialist Republic (SSR).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union)

🎖️ 1948 – Members of the Malayan Communist Party kill three British plantation managers in Sungai Siput; in response, British Malaya declares a state of emergency.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malayan_Communist_Party)

📡 2013 – A multi-day cloudburst, centered on the North Indian state of Uttarakhand, causes devastating floods and landslides, becoming the country's worst natural disaster since the 2004 tsunami.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2013_North_India_floods)

🏙️ 1940 – World War II: Marshal Henri Philippe Pétain becomes Chief of State of Vichy France (Chef de l'État Français).. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

⚙️ 1815 – Battle of Ligny and Battle of Quatre Bras, two days before the Battle of Waterloo.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Ligny)

💥 2019 – Upwards of 2,000,000 people participate in the 2019–20 Hong Kong protests, the largest in Hong Kong's history.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2019%E2%80%932020_Hong_Kong_protests)

🕊️ 2000 – The Secretary-General of the UN reports that Israel has complied with United Nations Security Council Resolution 425, 22 years after its issuance, and completely withdrawn from Lebanon. The Resolution does not encompass the Shebaa farms, which is claimed by Israel, Syria and Lebanon.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Security_Council_Resolution_425)

⚔️ 1897 – A treaty annexing the Republic of Hawaii to the United States is signed; the Republic would not be dissolved until a year later.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Hawaii)

🏰 2012 – The United States Air Force's robotic Boeing X-37B spaceplane returns to Earth after a classified 469-day orbital mission.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Air_Force)
