# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ 285 – Roman emperor Diocletian names Maximian his co-emperor ("Augustus").. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_emperor)

🏰 527 – Byzantine Emperor Justin I names his nephew Justinian I as co-ruler and successor to the throne.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Byzantine_emperors)

📜 1081 – Alexios I Komnenos overthrows the Byzantine emperor Nikephoros III Botaneiates, and, after his troops spend three days extensively looting Constantinople, is formally crowned on April 4.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexios_I_Komnenos)

🛡️ 1572 – In the Eighty Years' War, the Watergeuzen capture Brielle from the Seventeen Provinces, gaining the first foothold on land for what would become the Dutch Republic.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eighty_Years'_War)

⚓ 1725 – J. S. Bach's later Easter Oratorio in its first version is performed at the Nikolaikirche in Leipzig on Easter Sunday.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johann_Sebastian_Bach)

🚀 1954 – United States President Dwight D. Eisenhower authorizes the creation of the United States Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs, Colorado.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dwight_D._Eisenhower)

💼 1947 – The only mutiny in the history of the Royal New Zealand Navy begins.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1947_Royal_New_Zealand_Navy_mutinies)

🎖️ 2011 – After protests against the burning of the Quran turn violent, a mob attacks a United Nations compound in Mazar-i-Sharif, Afghanistan, resulting in the deaths of fourteen people, including seven UN workers.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dove_World_Outreach_Center_Quran-burning_controversy)

📡 1960 – The TIROS-1 satellite transmits the first television picture from space.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Television_Infrared_Observation_Satellite)

🏙️ 1933 – The recently elected Nazis under Julius Streicher organize a one-day boycott of all Jewish-owned businesses in Germany, ushering in a series of anti-Semitic acts.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazism)

⚙️ 1984 – Singer Marvin Gaye is shot to death by his father in his home in Arlington Heights, Los Angeles, California.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marvin_Gaye)

💥 1969 – The Hawker Siddeley Harrier, the first operational fighter aircraft with Vertical/Short Takeoff and Landing capabilities, enters service with the Royal Air Force.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hawker_Siddeley_Harrier)

🕊️ 1935 – India's central banking institution, the Reserve Bank of India, is formed.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reserve_Bank_of_India)

⚔️ 1986 – Communist Party of Nepal (Mashal) cadres attack a number of police stations in Kathmandu, seeking to incite a popular rebellion.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Communist_Party_of_Nepal_(Mashal))

🏰 1900 – Prince George becomes absolute monarch of the Cretan State.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_George_of_Greece_and_Denmark)
