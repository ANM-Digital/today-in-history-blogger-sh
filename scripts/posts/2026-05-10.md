# 🏛️ Today in History


⚔️ -28 – A sunspot is observed by Han dynasty astronomers during the reign of Emperor Cheng of Han, one of the earliest dated sunspot observations in China.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sunspot)

🏰 946 – Alberic II of Spoleto arranges the election of pope Agapetus II following the death of Pope Marinus II.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alberic_II_of_Spoleto)

📜 1291 – Scottish nobles recognize the authority of Edward I of England pending the selection of a king.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nobility)

🛡️ 1497 – Amerigo Vespucci allegedly leaves Cádiz for his first voyage to the New World.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amerigo_Vespucci)

⚓ 1503 – Christopher Columbus visits the Cayman Islands and names them Las Tortugas after the numerous turtles there.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christopher_Columbus)

🚀 1688 – King Narai nominates Phetracha as regent, leading to the revolution of 1688 in which Phetracha becomes king of the Ayutthaya Kingdom.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narai)

💼 1713 – Great Northern War: The Russian Navy led by Admiral Fyodor Apraksin land both at Katajanokka and Hietalahti during the Battle of Helsinki.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Northern_War)

🎖️ 2005 – A hand grenade thrown by Vladimir Arutyunian lands about 20 m from U.S. President George W. Bush while he is giving a speech to a crowd in Tbilisi, Georgia, but it malfunctions and does not detonate.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grenade)

📡 1904 – The Horch & Cir. Motorwagenwerke AG is founded. It would eventually become the Audi company.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horch)

🏙️ 1996 – A blizzard strikes Mount Everest, killing eight climbers by the next day.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mount_Everest)

⚙️ 1946 – First successful launch of an American V-2 rocket at White Sands Proving Ground.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/V-2_rocket)

💥 1940 – World War II: German fighters accidentally bomb the German city of Freiburg.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II)

🕊️ 2017 – Syrian civil war: The Syrian Democratic Forces (SDF) capture the last footholds of the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant (ISIL) in Al-Tabqah, bringing the Battle of Tabqa to an end.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syrian_civil_war)

⚔️ 1975 – Sony introduces the Betamax videocassette recorder.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sony)

🏰 1924 – J. Edgar Hoover is appointed first Director of the United States' Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), and remains so until his death in 1972.. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._Edgar_Hoover)
